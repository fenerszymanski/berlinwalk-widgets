Good to know: the paintings you see are 2009 repaints of the 1990 originals, on the inner side of the Wall. It is a protected memorial, so enjoy it, photograph it, but do not add your own graffiti. The East Side Gallery sits east of our free walking tour through Berlin's historic core.
