Berlin Train Gateways 2026

Between 14 June and 12 December 2026 the Stadtbahn, the elevated line that carries trains straight across the middle of Berlin, is closed to regional and long-distance trains. Departures moved. Tap a station to see what it does now, and switch the ticket you are holding to see which gateways it actually opens.
