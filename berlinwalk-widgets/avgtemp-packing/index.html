🧳 What to Pack for Berlin
🌸
Spring
Mar – May · 8–19°C
✅ Light jacket or blazer
✅ Layerable sweater/cardigan
✅ T-shirts + long sleeves
✅ Comfortable walking shoes
✅ Packable rain jacket
✅ Sunglasses
⚡ Key tip: Layers are everything — mornings cold, afternoons warm
☀️
Summer
Jun – Aug · 23–25°C
✅ Light clothing (shorts, dresses)
✅ Sunscreen SPF 30+
✅ Sun hat
✅ Reusable water bottle
✅ Comfortable sandals/sneakers
✅ Light rain jacket (thunderstorms!)
⚡ Key tip: Pack one evening layer — it cools after sunset
🍂
Autumn
Sep – Nov · 7–20°C
✅ Medium-weight jacket
✅ Warm sweater
✅ Jeans/long trousers
✅ Closed waterproof shoes
✅ Scarf (especially Nov)
✅ Umbrella
⚡ Key tip: September is still warm; November is pre-winter
❄️
Winter
Dec – Feb · 0–4°C
✅ Heavy winter coat
✅ Thermal underlayer
✅ Warm hat + gloves + scarf
✅ Waterproof insulated boots
✅ Thick socks (2 pairs/day)
✅ Hand warmers (optional)
⚡ Key tip: Waterproof boots are non-negotiable for walking tours
Our free walking tour is 1h 45min outdoors — dress for the weather! 🚶
