## Licence caveats to check before publishing

- The `bayerischesviertel-*` "Orte des Erinnerns" signs and `bowie-05` (Bowie bust) are photographs of **copyrighted artworks** (Stih & Schnock; street art). Commons hosts them under German freedom of panorama, which covers permanently installed public works, but the artwork itself is not freely licensed. Credit the artist as well as the photographer.
- Always reproduce the exact artist string plus the licence name and a link to the licence when publishing CC BY / CC BY-SA files.
