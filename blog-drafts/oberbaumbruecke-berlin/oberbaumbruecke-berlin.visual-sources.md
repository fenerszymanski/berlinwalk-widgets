# Oberbaumbrücke — Visual Sources

All images are Wikimedia Commons, verified licence + author on 2026-07-24.
Masters optimized to 1600px long edge, JPEG q86. Raw originals in `images/raw/`
(gitignored) can be rebuilt from the source URLs below.

## Final set (cover + 4 inline = 5 images)

1. **cover-oberbaumbruecke.jpg** — Oberbaumbrücke with a U-Bahn train crossing,
   a tour boat passing beneath, and the towers framed by trees. Strongest,
   most recognizable, topic-specific cover; reads clearly at card-crop size.
   - Author: Penguin9 · CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin_-_BVG_A3E_U-Bahn_auf_Oberbaumbr%C3%BCcke.jpg

2. **molecule-man-view.jpg** — The Molecule Man sculpture standing in the Spree
   with the Oberbaumbrücke and the TV Tower upstream. Used for the "look east
   from the bridge" detail.
   - Author: Paul Korecky · CC BY-SA 2.0
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:2018-08-12_DE_Berlin-Friedrichshain-Kreuzberg_%26_Berlin-Mitte,_Spree,_Molecule_Man,_Oberbaumbr%C3%BCcke,_Berliner_Fernsehturm_(50001756588).jpg

3. **tower-detail.jpg** — Neo-Gothic brick tower with the Friedrichshain-Kreuzberg
   crest, a modern high-rise behind it. Used for the "looks medieval, built 1896"
   / old-and-new section.
   - Author: Raimond Spekking · CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Detail_Oberbaumbr%C3%BCcke_Berlin,_high-rise_Treptower-1351.jpg

4. **blue-hour.jpg** — The lit bridge reflected in the Spree at blue hour. Used
   for the "best light / go at dusk" section.
   - Author: Michabka · CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin,_Oberbaumbr%C3%BCcke_in_der_blauen_Stunde_mit_Spiegelung.jpg

5. **border-1992.jpg** — Aerial of the Oberbaumbrücke and the Spree in 1992,
   just after reconstruction began and the border years ended. Used for the
   division/reopening history section.
   - Author: Throttle lever · CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:1992-09-10_Oberbaumbr%C3%BCcke.jpg
