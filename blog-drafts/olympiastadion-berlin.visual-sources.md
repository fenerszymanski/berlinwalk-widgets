## Selected (5)

1. **01-olympiastadion-berlin-interior-bowl-cover.jpg** (COVER)
   - File: Olympiastadion Berlin Innenansicht.jpg
   - Author: Jan Künzel — License: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Olympiastadion_Berlin_Innenansicht.jpg
   - Why: The Olympic-flame cauldron on its pillars at the Marathon Gate, framed by the roof opening with the pitch beyond. Distinctive, architectural, instantly reads as Olympiastadion at card size.

2. **02-olympiastadion-berlin-night-floodlit.jpg**
   - File: Berliner Olympiastadion night.jpg
   - Author: Tobi 87 — License: CC BY 3.0
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berliner_Olympiastadion_night.jpg
   - Why: The bowl full and floodlit for an athletics night. Shows the stadium alive, supports the "see a match/event" section.

3. **03-olympiastadion-berlin-exterior-facade.jpg**
   - File: Berlin Jun 2012 026 (Olympiastadion).JPG
   - Author: Michael Barera — License: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin_Jun_2012_026_(Olympiastadion).JPG
   - Why: The twin east-gate towers with the Olympic rings hanging between them. The recognisable public face of the stadium.

4. **04-olympiastadion-berlin-aerial-olympic-park.jpg**
   - File: Olympiastadion Berlin (Luftbild).jpg
   - Author: Olaf Tausch — License: CC BY 3.0
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Olympiastadion_Berlin_(Luftbild).jpg
   - Why: Aerial showing the stadium set in the green parkland of the Olympic Park in the west, reinforcing the "trip out west" framing.

5. **05-glockenturm-bell-tower-maifeld-olympiapark.jpg**
