# Wannsee Berlin — source verification

Internal research record for the 21 July 2026 draft. Public copy must keep
provider, production and workflow notes out of the article and image credits.

## Official visitor facts

- **Strandbad Wannsee:** Berliner Bäder lists the 29 June–30 August 2026 block
  as daily 09:00–20:00. Standard admission is 6.50 EUR, reduced 3.80 EUR. The
  12 EUR Badespass ticket covers three people with at least one adult and one
  child; additional children aged five or older cost 1.50 EUR each, up to six
  people total and no more than two adults. Under-fives enter free. The operator
  describes 1,275 metres of sand, about 80 metres depth and capacity above
  30,000. Source:
  <https://www.berlinerbaeder.de/baeder/detail/strandbad-wannsee/>
- **Liebermann-Villa:** April–September hours are 10:00–18:00, closed Tuesday;
  admission is 12 EUR. Bus 114 is the direct public-transport connection.
  Source: <https://liebermann-villa.de/en/visit/your-visit/>
- **House of the Wannsee Conference:** the exhibition and garden are normally
  open daily 10:00–18:00 apart from the site's listed annual closure dates.
  Entry is free; individuals do not need to book. The physical audio guide is
  3 EUR and the web app is free. Sources: <https://www.ghwk.de/en/your-visit>
  and <https://www.ghwk.de/en/education/tours>
- **Pfaueninsel:** the summer island ferry normally runs 10:00–18:00 at
  20-minute intervals. The island ticket is 6 EUR, reduced 5 EUR and family
  12 EUR. The palace is normally Tuesday–Sunday 10:00–17:30 and interior entry
  is by German-language guided tour for 8 EUR. SPSG's current notice lists no
  visitor ferry on 21, 27, 30 and 31 July 2026. The official page confirms
  free-roaming peacocks but does not support a current exact bird count.
  Source: <https://www.spsg.de/schloesser-gaerten/objekt/schloss-pfaueninsel>
- **F10 ferry:** BVG lists an approximately 20-minute crossing and hourly
  service. For 1 May–30 August 2026 the published service window is roughly
  06:00–21:00 Monday–Friday, 07:00–21:00 Saturday and 09:00–21:00 Sunday.
  Passengers must leave the vessel at the terminus, and a short-trip ticket is
  not valid. Source: <https://www.bvg.de/de/verbindungen/linienuebersicht/f10>
- **Berlin AB fare:** the single ticket is 4.00 EUR and covers one journey in
  one direction for 120 minutes. Return and round trips are not permitted. This
  is why the article now says that AB fare applies, not that one single ticket
  covers an entire Wannsee day. Source:
  <https://www.bvg.de/en/subscriptions-and-tickets/all-tickets/single-tickets/single-ticket>
- **Bus 114:** the official line page and timetable place Liebermann-Villa two
  stops before Haus der Wannsee-Konferenz, not three. Source:
  <https://www.bvg.de/de/verbindungen/linienuebersicht/114>

## Editorial corrections made after verification

- Removed the unsupported exact count of 55 peacocks.
- Removed the claim that summer S-Bahn engineering does not affect the line;
  readers are told to check the live S-Bahn or BVG service instead.
- Removed the stale “two euros and change” F10 line.
- Qualified the Pfaueninsel ferry timetable and added the current July closure
  dates.
- Replaced recurring first-person visitor anecdotes that could not be evidenced
  with direct, place-first advice.
- Moved image attribution from an open article block to the planner's compact,
  default-closed Image credits dialog.
