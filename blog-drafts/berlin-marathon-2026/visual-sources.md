### 1. cover-strasse-des-17-juni-finish.jpg — FEATURED / COVER
- Commons: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin-Marathon_-_Ziel_-_Brandenburger_Tor_-_Foto-_H._Milde.jpg
- Direct: https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/52/Berlin-Marathon_-_Ziel_-_Brandenburger_Tor_-_Foto-_H._Milde.jpg
- Licence: CC BY-SA 4.0 · Author: Mischelbach
- Original 4032x3024 → 1600x1200
- Shows: the 2025 finish straight looking east down Stra&szlig;e des 17. Juni, with
  the full field of runners, packed spectator barriers on both sides, the
  Brandenburg Gate and the TV Tower in the frame.
- Why cover: it is the one image that says "the marathon has taken the middle of
  Berlin" without a caption. Two instantly recognisable landmarks, autumn light,
  and the density of the field all read at listing-card size. It also matches
  the article's actual subject, which is the city rather than the race.

## Rejected, with reasons

- `1000 - Ziel Berlin Marathon.jpg` (CC BY-SA 4.0, 2601x1765) — the obvious
  Brandenburg Gate finish shot, but a large beer advertising column dominates the
  right edge and the runners are small. Weaker than the chosen cover for the
  same job, and the ad makes it look like a sponsor photo.
- `Berlin Marathon 2019 136/162/199.jpg` — the whole 2019 set is grey, wet and
  flat. Legally fine, editorially dull, and a rainy frame would misrepresent a
  late-September Berlin morning as the default.
- `Music at Berlin-Marathon on Hauptstraße ... 01.jpg` (CC0) — heavy motion blur
  and a cluttered underpass. The idea was good, the frame is not.
- `Berlin Marathon 2018 011/263/615.jpg`, `Berlin Marathon 2015 ...`,
  `Berlin Marathon 20170924 ...` — generic runners against featureless
  backdrops. Nothing in the frame says Berlin, so they add no information.
- `Berlin-Marathon main run on Kleiststraße ... 01.jpg` (CC0) — sparse, almost
  no runners, no story despite the interesting U-Bahn trench.
- `Berlin Marathon 2022 Teilnehmerfeld Unter den Linden Brandenburger Tor.jpg` —
  portrait crop, hazy, and the Gate is small. The chosen cover does this job
  better.
- `Berlin inlinemarathon riesengrppe.JPG` — harsh, low quality.
- `Berlin Marathon 2009 Alt Moabit.JPG` — dated and ordinary.
- `Berlin-Marathon ... 2024-09-29` frames 02, 03, 04, 06, 09, and the wheelchair
  series — all usable and all CC0, but redundant next to the five stronger picks
  from the same set. Kept in `images/cand/` for reference.
