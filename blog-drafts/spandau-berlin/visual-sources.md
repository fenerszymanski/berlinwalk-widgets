## Selected (7)

1. `cover-zitadelle-aerial.jpg` — FEATURED/COVER
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Aerial_image_of_Spandau_Citadel_(view_from_the_southeast).jpg
   - Author: Carsten Steger — License: CC BY-SA 4.0
     (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/)
   - Why: the whole article in one frame. Star-fort geometry, moat, Juliusturm
     all readable at listing-card size; summer greens carry the brand palette.
2. `zitadelle-bastion-altstadt.jpg`
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Zitadelle_B-Spandau_07-2017_img2.jpg
   - Author: A.Savin — License: FAL (Free Art License,
     http://artlibre.org/licence/lal/en/)
   - Why: bastion prow in front, Altstadt and Havel behind; shows how close
     the fortress sits to the old town.
3. `zitadelle-gatehouse-crest.jpg`
