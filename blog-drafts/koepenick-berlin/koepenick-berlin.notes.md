## Verified facts (sources checked 2026-07-23)

- Köpenick first mentioned 1209 as "Copnic"; older than Cölln (1237) and
  Berlin (1244). Slavic settlement at the confluence of Dahme and Spree.
  Sources: https://www.berlin.de/ba-treptow-koepenick/ueber-den-bezirk/ortsteile/koepenick/artikel.450127.php ,
  https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/K%C3%B6penick
- Schloss Köpenick: Dutch Baroque, built 1677-1685; today a branch of the
  Kunstgewerbemuseum (Staatliche Museen zu Berlin). Summer (Apr-Sep):
  Wed-Fri 11-17, Sat-Sun 11-18; winter (Oct-Mar): Thu-Sun 11-17. Closed
  Mon-Tue. Admission EUR 8 / reduced EUR 4. Address Schlossinsel 1.
  Sources: https://www.smb.museum/en/museums-institutions/schloss-koepenick/plan-your-visit/opening-hours/ ,
  https://www.smb.museum/en/museums-institutions/schloss-koepenick/plan-your-visit/prices-tickets/
- 28 Oct 1730: royal court-martial met in the palace's coat-of-arms hall to
  try Crown Prince Frederick (later Frederick the Great) and Lieutenant Hans
  Hermann von Katte after their attempted flight to England.
  Source: https://www.berlin-tourismus-online.de/250.html (and SMB history pages).
- Hauptmann von Köpenick: on 16 October 1906 unemployed shoemaker Friedrich
  Wilhelm Voigt (13.2.1849-3.1.1922), dressed as a Prussian captain,
  commandeered soldiers, occupied Rathaus Köpenick, arrested the mayor and
  took ~4,000 marks from the town treasury; the stunt entered German as
  "Köpenickiade"; Carl Zuckmayer play 1931. Berlin memorial plaque at the
  Rathaus entrance; bronze captain statue by Spartak Babajan at the Rathaus
  steps since 1996.
  Sources: https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hauptmann_von_K%C3%B6penick ,
  https://www.berlin.de/museum-treptow-koepenick/gedenkorte/artikel.801627.php ,
  https://www.berlin.de/ba-treptow-koepenick/aktuelles/pressemitteilungen/2021/pressemitteilung.1153348.php
- Rathaus Köpenick: brick Gothic-revival town hall (1901-1905 era), Alt-Köpenick 21.
- Müggelsee: Berlin's largest lake, ~7.3 km² water surface, ~4.5 km long,
  ~2.5 km wide; districts Köpenick, Friedrichshagen, Rahnsdorf, Müggelheim
  border it. Sources: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/M%C3%BCggelsee ,
  https://www.visitberlin.de/en/lake-muggelsee
- Müggelturm: 30 m tower on the Kleiner Müggelberg (88 m), 126 steps,
  adult EUR 6 / reduced EUR 3, hours listed 10:00-16:00 (short; advise
  checking ahead). Source: https://www.berlin.de/en/attractions-and-sights/3560054-3104052-mueggelturm.en.html
- Ferry F12: BVG ferry Grünau-Wendenschloß across the Dahme, ~2 minute
  crossing, year-round, normal BVG ticket. Tram 68 connects Schloßplatz with
  the Wendenschloß terminus and also serves Wassersportallee on the Grünau
  side, so the same line closes the ferry loop. Tram 62 does not serve
  Wendenschloß. Sources:
  https://www.berlin.de/en/public-transportation/ferries/1826182-7322324-ferry-f12.en.html ,
  https://www.bvg.de/en/connections/route-overview/f12 ,
  https://www.bvg.de/en/connections/station-overview/schlossplatz-koepenick ,
  https://www.bvg.de/en/connections/route-overview/68 ,
  https://www.bvg.de/en/connections/route-overview/62
- Arrival from S Köpenick: bus 164 serves Schloßplatz Köpenick directly in
  about nine minutes. Neither tram 62 nor tram 68 connects S Köpenick station
  to Schloßplatz. Source:
  https://www.bvg.de/en/connections/route-overview/164
- S3 status July 2026: S3 serves Berlin-Köpenick station; only Hirschgarten
  is skipped 10 Jul 22:00 - 10 Aug 04:00, 2026 (bus S3H Hirschgarten-Köpenick).
  In the normal stopping pattern Friedrichshagen is two stops east of
  Köpenick, with Hirschgarten between them. The evergreen public copy avoids
  a stop count because the temporary closure changes it.
  Köpenick station is being rebuilt into a regional station; DB currently
  targets December 2027 for regional trains to stop there.
  Sources: https://www.entwicklungsstadt.de/s-bahnhof-hirschgarten-deutsche-bahn-kuendigt-neue-sperrungen-an/ ,
  https://www.entwicklungsstadt.de/umbau-des-bahnhofs-koepenick-neue-gleistrasse-und-regionalbahnhof-bis-2027/
- Union Berlin: Stadion An der Alten Försterei, An der Wuhlheide (covered in
  our football-match-in-berlin post; capacity 22,012 there).
- Köpenick is in Berlin fare zone B: a normal AB ticket covers everything in
  this post including the F12 ferry.
