## Official Sources Checked

- BVG Lost & Found:
  - URL: https://www.bvg.de/en/service-and-support/lost-and-found
  - Checked facts: online lost items report, detailed description, request active for several weeks, bulk items not maintained electronically, BVG Fundbüro address Rudolfstraße 1-8, 10245 Berlin-Friedrichshain, near U Warschauer Straße, listed hours Monday/Tuesday/Thursday/Friday 9:00-17:00, Wednesday/weekend/holidays closed.
- Berlin.de lost-and-found overview:
  - URL: https://www.berlin.de/en/tourism/travel-information/1735948-2862820-lost-and-found-and-lost-property-offices.en.html
  - Checked facts: central lost property office handles most lost/found city items, address Platz der Luftbrücke 6, missing passport/ID must be reported to police immediately, visitors needing travel documents should contact embassy, public transport lost-property split.
- Deutsche Bahn lost & found:
  - URL: https://int.bahn.de/en/help/contact/lost-luggage
  - Checked facts: online report for items worth more than EUR 15, item may arrive after hours or days, handling fee for collecting/sending lost item.
- BER Airport lost items:
  - URL: https://ber.berlin-airport.de/en/flying/baggage/gegenstand-verloren.html
  - Checked facts: contact depends on where the item was lost; terminal/security/arrivals items go to BER Lost Property Office at Service Centre, Terminal 1 Level U1; airline handles items on plane/apron bus and damaged/lost baggage; transport companies handle trains/buses; Federal/State Police Joint Service Point handles documents for a short period and foreign citizens should contact consulate.
- Berlin Police tourist page:
  - URL: https://www.berlin.de/en/tourism/travel-information/1749512-2862820-police.en.html
  - Checked facts: police emergency number 110.
- visitBerlin Help + advice:
  - URL: https://www.visitberlin.de/en/help-advice
  - Checked facts: emergency numbers context and tourist help framing.
