# Friedrichshain — Visual Sources

Post: `friedrichshain-berlin` (Tourist Tips). All images are real photographs from
Wikimedia Commons under CC licenses. No AI-generated visuals were used in this
post. Reader-facing attribution is in the article's "Image credits" block.

Selection bar: strong, topic-specific, instantly readable at card size; reject
weak/cluttered/misleading images. A local contact sheet was reviewed by opening
each candidate full-size before selection.

## Used (6)

1. **Cover — East Side Gallery (Brotherhood Kiss)**
   - File: `optimized/cover-eastside-gallery.jpg`
   - Source: `File:East Side Gallery - Dmitri Vrubel - Le baiser (Berlin).jpg`
   - Photographer: Gzen92 — License: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Why: the single most recognizable Friedrichshain image; iconic, sharp,
     instantly readable as a listing card. Cover + inline in the East Side section.

2. **Oberbaumbrücke**
   - File: `optimized/oberbaumbruecke.jpg`
   - Source: `File:Oberbaum Bridge, Berlin 03.jpg` — Ank Kumar — CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Why: iconic double-deck brick bridge linking Friedrichshain and Kreuzberg,
     blue sky, clean composition.

3. **RAW-Gelände (dusk)**
   - File: `optimized/raw-gelaende.jpg`
   - Source: `File:Berlin RAW-Gelände 2020.jpg` — DavideGorla — CC BY 2.0
   - Why: captures the yard perfectly (Skatehalle, Photoautomat, graffiti,
     industrial dusk). Authentic, not staged.

4. **Boxhagener Platz flea market**
   - File: `optimized/boxhagener-flohmarkt.jpg`
   - Source: `File:Berlin Boxhagener Pl pchli targ.jpg` — Andrzej Otrębski — CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Why: real Sunday flea-market crowd on the square. Bare trees (off-season) but
     clearly and authentically the market; used inline, not as cover.

5. **Volkspark Friedrichshain — Märchenbrunnen**
   - File: `optimized/volkspark-maerchenbrunnen.jpg`
   - Source: `File:Märchenbrunnen (Berlin-Friedrichshain).jpg` — Lukas Beck — CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Why: bright, summery fountain shot; strong green-space image for a summer post.

6. **Frankfurter Tor / Karl-Marx-Allee**
   - File: `optimized/frankfurter-tor-karl-marx-allee.jpg`
   - Source: `File:Berlin Karl-Marx-Allee Frankfurter Tor 2.jpg` — Andreas Praefcke — CC BY 3.0
   - Why: clean blue-sky view of the distinctive twin-domed socialist gateway.

## Rejected

  an artist's signature/email at the base of a mural, not a usable gallery view.
