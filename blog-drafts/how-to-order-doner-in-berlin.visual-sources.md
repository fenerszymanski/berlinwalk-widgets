## Editorial rationale
- Cover: the Gemüse Kebap close-up is the exact Berlin-signature product the
  post explains (fried veg + mint + feta), instantly readable at card size,
  appetizing, not a generic storefront. Clears the featured-image bar.
- doner-im-brot: shows the classic bread version for the "döner im Brot vs
  dürüm" section.
- duerum-wrap: shows the dürüm/wrap form so readers can picture the choice.
- doner-plate-onions: supports the "mit alles / ohne Zwiebeln" salad + onion
  ordering section.
- mustafas-queue: real Berlin street scene at the most-hyped kiosk; supports
  the "expect a wait, and it is not the only good döner" judgment.

- Older 2010 low-res Berlin döner (1445x963) — weaker than the CC BY-SA 4.0 set.
- Generic non-Berlin plate/kebab shots — less place-specific than the chosen set.
