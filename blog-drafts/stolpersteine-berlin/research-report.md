# Stolpersteine Berlin — research report (internal, 2026-07-20)

Structured findings from the drafting-day research pass. Facts marked
VERIFIED were seen on current authoritative pages on 2026-07-20; UNVERIFIED
items were deliberately kept out of the public article.

## Key verified facts used in the article
- Artist Gunter Demnig, b. 27 Oct 1947 Berlin; concept published 1993; single
  decree stone Cologne City Hall 16 Dec 1992; regular layings mid-90s.
  (stolpersteine.eu artist page; en.wikipedia; AP May 2026)
- Berlin first stones: May/June 1996, Kreuzberg, 51 stones around
  Oranienstr./Dresdener Str., laid WITHOUT permission during NGBK exhibition,
  retroactively legalized. First: Lina Friedemann, Oranienstr. 158.
  (de.wikipedia Liste der Stolpersteine in Berlin-Kreuzberg; visitberlin.de)
  2026 marks 30 years — NO official citywide jubilee found; do not claim one.
  Stolperstein-Konferenz 2026 exists (berlin.de politische-bildung page).
- Counts: 126,000+ stones, 31 countries (AP 7 May 2026). Berlin: 11,842 as of
  May 2026 per de-Wikipedia; official DB shows ~10,947 records (lags) → use
  "more than 11,000". District counts: Charlottenburg-Wilmersdorf 4,303;
  Mitte 2,197; Tempelhof-Schöneberg 1,390 (Schöneberg 997);
  Friedrichshain-Kreuzberg 1,256 (Kreuzberg 781).
  100,000th stone: 26 May 2023 Nuremberg (Johann Wild).
- Formula: HIER WOHNTE (variants HIER LEBTE/LERNTE/LEHRTE/ARBEITETE/
  PRAKTIZIERTE/WIRKTE); JG. = Jahrgang; fate lines DEPORTIERT/ERMORDET/TOT;
  FLUCHT IN DEN TOD is the project's chosen wording. 96 mm brass plate on
  concrete cube; one stone = one person; hand-stamped by Michael
  Friedrichs-Friedländer's workshop (~450-600/mo, still active 2026).
  Last freely chosen residence rule ("letzter frei gewählter Wohnort").
  Stolperschwelle = larger threshold format for groups (Berlin has 8).
- All victim groups of Nazism, not only Jewish Berliners.
- Sponsoring: EUR 120 Germany / EUR 132 abroad (official FAQ). Berlin wait:
  months to years; UP TO FOUR YEARS in Charlottenburg-Wilmersdorf,
  Tempelhof-Schöneberg, Mitte. Anyone can propose; district initiatives +
  Koordinierungsstelle Stolpersteine Berlin coordinate. New dates with Demnig
  bookable again from 2026 (official news 14 Jan 2025).
- Etiquette: walking over them is officially FINE (Demnig: like cathedral
  grave slabs; footsteps polish). Cleaning: drugstore metal/brass polish on a
  cloth + water + sponge, NO wire brush; Nov 9 polishing tradition
  (Pogromnacht anniversary), visitBerlin lists cleaning events.
- Places: Otto Weidt workshop Rosenthaler Str. 39 (free museum, stones for
  murdered workers); Große Hamburger Str. Missing House (SEPARATE memorial,
  do not conflate); Koppenplatz Deserted Room; Bayerisches Viertel
  (997 stones + separate Orte des Erinnerns lamppost signs);
  Kreuzberg origin cluster. Official searchable map/database + free
  "Stolpersteine in Berlin" app (iOS/Android, Koordinierungsstelle).
- Munich bans stones on public ground (2004, upheld 2015; Knobloch
  objection); wall plaques there instead. "World's largest decentralized
  memorial" phrasing is safe.
- Demnig, 78, still lays stones personally (AP May 2026).
