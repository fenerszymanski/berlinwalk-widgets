## Source Notes

- Official Sachsenhausen opening/prices/travel page:
  `https://www.sachsenhausen-sbg.de/en/visitor-service/opening-times-prices-and-travel-information/`
  - Visitor Information Centre daily 8:30-17:00.
  - Outdoor areas/exhibitions summer 8:30-18:00, winter 8:30-16:30.
  - Admission to memorial and museums free.
  - Audio guide EUR 3.50 per device.
  - S1 to Oranienburg, about 45 minutes from Friedrichstrasse.
  - RE5 from Hauptbahnhof to Oranienburg, about 25 minutes, hourly.
  - Bus 804 and 821 from Oranienburg station to `Gedenkstätte`.
  - Berlin ABC from Hauptbahnhof/Friedrichstrasse, Berlin BC from Lichtenberg.
- Official offers for individual visitors:
  `https://www.sachsenhausen-sbg.de/en/visitor-service/offers-for-individual-visitors/`
  - Saturday public guided tours: 11:00 English, 14:00 German.
  - Tour approximately 2 hours.
  - Recommended for visitors 14+.
  - On-site registration at Visitor Information Centre; no advance mail/phone registration.
- Official permanent exhibitions:
  `https://www.sachsenhausen-sbg.de/en/exhibitions/permanent-exhibitions/`
  - Decentralised museum concept with thirteen exhibitions.
  - Tower A, Barracks 38/39, prisoners' kitchen, Station Z, Soviet Special Camp and other exhibition anchors.
- Official 1936-1945 history:
  `https://www.sachsenhausen-sbg.de/en/history/1936-1945-sachsenhausen-concentration-camp/`
  - Built in summer 1936 as model camp.
  - More than 200,000 people imprisoned 1936-1945.
  - Tens of thousands died; at least 10,000 Soviet prisoners of war murdered in autumn 1941.
  - Liberated on 22 April 1945.
- Official 1945-1950 Soviet Special Camp history:
  `https://www.sachsenhausen-sbg.de/en/history/1945-1950-soviet-special-camp/`
  - Around 60,000 internees; about 12,000 died of hunger and disease.
- Official accessibility:
  `https://www.sachsenhausen-sbg.de/en/visitor-service/accessibility/`
  - Accessibility is possible only to a limited extent; accompaniment recommended for people with mobility/visual impairments.
  - Wheelchair accessible toilets and loan wheelchairs are noted.
- Berlin.de ticket zones/fares:
  `https://www.berlin.de/en/public-transportation/1772016-2913840-tickets-fares-and-route-maps.en.html`
  - ABC includes Berlin surrounding area.
  - 2026 ABC single ticket EUR 5.00, ABC 24-hour ticket EUR 12.90.
