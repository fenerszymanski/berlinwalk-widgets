## Rejected (with reasons)

- 2019-08-06 Karl-Marx-Allee (Weberwiese U entry): flat composition, dull sky.
- 2019-08-08 aerial from Fernsehturm: hazy, boulevard reads thin at card size.
- Karl-Marx-Allee 56-68 Dec 2019: bare winter trees, parked-car clutter.
- Frankfurter Tor November 2013 / south+north tower 2013: redundant single-tower
  crops, cover already carries the towers.
- Hochhaus an der Weberwiese (both): building mostly hidden behind trees.
- Karl-Marx-Allee 12.09.20 -1/-2: roadworks barriers, sterile empty asphalt.
- New year 2021 CC0 set: night shots, low information value for this angle.
- Fortepan 50924 (1962): softer than the 1970 frame; one archival is enough.
- Kino International 2024 (01/02): scaffolding/renovation banner would
  misrepresent the reopened 2026 state.
- Frankfurter Tor U-Bahnhof series (CC BY-SA 2.0): platform interiors,
  off-topic for the boulevard walk.
