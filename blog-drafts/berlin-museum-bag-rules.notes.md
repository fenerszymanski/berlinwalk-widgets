Fact checked on 2026-07-03:

- SMB/Museum Island rules: bags/backpacks larger than 30 x 20 x 10 cm must be checked; storage options for bags/backpacks/suitcases are limited; items bigger than carry-on luggage cannot be accommodated.
- Humboldt Forum: bags larger than A4 should go to cloakroom/lockers; cloakroom free; house rules warn storage for bags/backpacks/suitcases is limited and larger-than-carry-on luggage cannot be accepted.
- Topography of Terror: lockers for carry-on luggage are available in the foyer area.
- Jewish Museum Berlin: security screening is mandatory; coats, umbrellas, large bags and backpacks must be left at cloakroom.
- DB Hauptbahnhof locker page lists sizes, quantities, prices, coin payment and 2-hour/24-hour durations.
