## Considered and rejected

- `c02-olympiastadion-matchday` — Dated 2007 Hertha vs Dortmund shot with a half-empty upper tier. Legally fine, visually weak next to the choreo cover.
- `c04-alte-foersterei-entrance` — Tram tracks and parked cars, no sense of the stadium. Dull.
- `c08-aufstieg-terrace` — Pitch churned to sand after the 2019 promotion. Reads as a damaged pitch, not a matchday.
- `c09-olympiastadion-night` — Packed and striking, but it is an athletics night, not football. Would have been misleading in a football article.
- `c12-koepenick-station` — Generic covered platform, no orientation value. Replaced by the Rathaus Köpenick departure board.
- `c13-koepenick-sbahn` — A station kiosk. Off topic.
- `c14-olympiastadion-ext` — Near-duplicate of the interior shot already used.
- `c15-foersterei-2007` — Only 1600x1200 and dated. Below the master ceiling once cropped.
