# Visual sources — Best Rooftop Bars in Berlin

All images are genuine, license-verified Wikimedia Commons photographs, reused
from earlier BerlinWalk posts where the license and provenance were already
checked. No AI-generated or code-drawn visuals were used in this post. Optimized
files (long edge 1600px, JPEG) live in `images/optimized/`.

Honesty note: Berlin's specific rooftop-bar interiors are hard to source at
strong editorial quality under a free license. Rather than mislabel generic
photos as specific named bars, the inline images show either a real rooftop bar
(Klunkerkranich) or the actual view/area each rooftop looks out over, and every
caption says what the image really is.

## Cover choice
`cover-berlin-skyline-golden.jpg` is the featured/cover image: a warm, clear,
high-angle skyline with the TV Tower rising over Mitte. It reads instantly as
"a great high view over Berlin" at listing-card crop, which is exactly the
promise of a rooftop-bar guide.

## Reader-facing Image Credits
All six images use CC BY / CC BY-SA licenses that require attribution, so a
reader-facing **Image credits** block is included at the foot of the published
body.

## Rejected
- Generic non-Berlin "sky bar" stock and hotel PR shots — either off-topic,
  unlicensed for reuse, or misleading if labelled as a specific venue.
- Tempelhofer Feld / Drachenberg sunset frames — beautiful but ground-level, not
  a rooftop feeling; kept out to avoid confusing the topic.
