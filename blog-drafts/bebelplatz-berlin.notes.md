## Why This Topic

Inventory checked through Wix Blog API plus local `blog-drafts`, Quick Summary, FAQ and tools-hub data. Existing BerlinWalk content mentions Bebelplatz inside Unter den Linden and seasonal/event guides, but no dedicated Bebelplatz article/tool was found. The topic is high-intent for visitors already near Unter den Linden, Museum Island and the BerlinWalk route, and it balances the recent practical transport/museum run with a Berlin History article.

Rejected overlaps:
- Berlin Wall and Cold War topics: already crowded by East Side Gallery, Wall remnants, Checkpoint Charlie, Topography of Terror and Tränenpalast coverage.
- Cash/card, late arrival, ticket zones, museum bag rules, plug adapter and transport backups: already covered by recent Tourist Tips articles.

## Search Intent

Visitors want to know what Bebelplatz is, where the book-burning memorial is, whether it is worth stopping, how long to spend, what they should look at, and how to fit it into Unter den Linden/Museum Island planning.
