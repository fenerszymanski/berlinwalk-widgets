## Quick Summary

- Berlin's Christmas market season for 2026 officially kicks off on **November 23, 2026**, with some early openings like the **Lichtenberger Winterzeit** (October 31) and the **RAW Medieval Market** (November 12).
- **WeihnachtsZauber Gendarmenmarkt** is returning to Gendarmenmarkt in 2026 (after years at Bebelplatz during renovations), featuring high-end crafts, gourmet food, and a €2 entry fee (free on weekdays before 2 PM).
- For a historical/family atmosphere, the **Berliner Weihnachtszeit am Roten Rathaus** features a Neptune Fountain ice rink and a giant Ferris wheel, running from November 23, 2026 to January 3, 2027.
- For a unique theme or alternative vibe, try the Scandinavian-themed **Lucia Christmas Market** at Kulturbrauerei, the **Heissa Holzmarkt** riverside village, or the **Klunkerkranich** rooftop market.
- Key tourist tips: always carry **cash** (many stalls do not accept cards), expect to pay a **Pfand** (deposit) of €3-5 on mugs, and don't skip the **Handbrot** (stuffed hot bread) or **Glühwein** (mulled wine).
