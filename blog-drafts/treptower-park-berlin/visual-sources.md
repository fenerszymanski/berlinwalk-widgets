## Notes
- Contact-sheet judgment done by viewing each candidate at ~1600px before selection.
- The two red granite flags are often rumoured to be built from stone salvaged
  from Hitler's Reich Chancellery. Per Wikipedia this is a myth ("it was rumoured
  ... but this was not the case"), so the article debunks rather than repeats it.
