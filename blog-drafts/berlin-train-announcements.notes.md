## Verified phrases + facts (with sources checked 2026-07-14)
1. **"Zurückbleiben, bitte!"** = "Stand back, please" — the U-Bahn's signature call as doors close.
   Still the U-Bahn announcement today. (Tripadvisor forums; iamexpat; common knowledge)
2. **"Einsteigen, bitte."** = "Please board / all aboard." Heard with the destination call.
3. **"Zug nach [destination]"** = "Train to [destination]." Destination = terminus = your direction check.
4. **"Nächster Halt: [station]."** = "Next stop: [station]." S-Bahn VoIP system announces line number,
   destination, next stop, and connections. (Wikipedia Berlin S-Bahn; sbahn.berlin)
5. **"Ausstieg rechts / links"** (sometimes "in Fahrtrichtung rechts/links") = "Exit on the right / left"
   (in the direction of travel). S-Bahn announces the exit side at every stop. (multiple forums; belatone)
6. **"Bitte beachten Sie beim Aussteigen die Lücke zwischen Zug und Bahnsteigkante!"** =
   "When getting off, mind the gap between the train and the platform edge." (some lines)
7. **The S-Bahn door jingle "doo-DOO-doo" (notes G–B–G)** — in use since 1986 on the East Berlin S-Bahn.
   Being phased out: an EU rail-safety rule requires fast pulsing tones (6–10 pulses/sec); new 483/484
   trains (from 2021) beep instead; older trains phased out into the mid-2030s. (iamexpat, 2024-era news;
   EU law signed 2014). Timely hook.
8. **"Schienenersatzverkehr" / "Ersatzverkehr (SEV)"** = rail replacement bus. A line/section is closed
   (planned construction or unplanned fault); purple buses marked "Ersatzverkehr / Replacement Service"
   follow the route, roughly every 3–5 min. (nuberlin; sbahn.berlin timetable changes; bvg)
9. Disruption vocabulary: **"Störung"** = fault/disruption; **"Verspätung"** = delay;
   **"Gleiswechsel"** = platform/track change; **"Bitte beachten Sie ..."** = "Please note ...".
10. **"Endstation"** = end of the line / terminus; **"bitte aussteigen"** = please get off.
11. Two operators/voices: **U-Bahn = BVG** (sparser: destination + Zurückbleiben; newer trains add next stop);
    **S-Bahn = DB / S-Bahn Berlin** (chattier: next stop + exit side + jingle + connections).
