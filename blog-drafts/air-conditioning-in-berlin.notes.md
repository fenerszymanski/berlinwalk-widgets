## Facts + sources (checked 2026-07-10)
- Berlin July highs typically ~22-30C, lows ~13-18C; heat comes in short
  episodes. Late-June 2026 heatwave set a preliminary German record ~41.8C,
  eased by early July. (weather2travel/AccuWeather/The Berliner summaries.)
- <5% of German homes have active AC; ownership survey ~18% in 2025 (up from 13%
  in 2023); "about one in eight households." (search: German AC penetration.)
- Rollladen (external shutters) block solar radiation before it hits the glass;
  exterior shading cuts cooling load ~25-40%. Stosslueften = burst cross-vent.
- BVG: U-Bahn trains are NOT air conditioned (ventilation only); buses/most
  trams have limited cooling. Source: https://nachgefragt.bvg.de/air-conditioning/
  (verified live 2026-07-10).
- German national warnings incl. heat: https://warnung.bund.de/ (verified exists).
- Hotel AC: chains/newer (Motel One, Adina, larger Steigenberger) usually yes;
  small/old-building stays often no; use booking AC filter + confirm by email.
