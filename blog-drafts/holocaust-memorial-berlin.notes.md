## Current facts used

- The Field of Stelae is free and accessible 24 hours a day.
- The underground Information Centre is free and, after reopening on 2026-05-01, regular hours are Tuesday to Sunday, 10:00-18:00; last admission is 45 minutes before closing.
- The Information Centre is normally closed Mondays and listed holiday/closure periods; readers are told to check the official page on the day.
- Official visitor rules: cross the Field of Stelae only on foot and slowly; no jumping from stele to stele, noise, pets, bicycles/equipment storage, smoking or alcohol.
- Accessibility: 13 marked corridors are suitable for wheelchair users; the Information Centre has lift access with staff support.
- Exhibition is recommended by the foundation for young people from 14.
