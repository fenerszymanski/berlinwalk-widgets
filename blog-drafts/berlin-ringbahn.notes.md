## Verified facts (sources below)
- 27 stations, ~37 km, full circle ~59–60 min. Shape nicknamed **"Hundekopf" (dog's head)**.
- **S41 = clockwise, S42 = anticlockwise** (circular S41/S42 branding since 28 May 2006). Full ring reopened **16 June 2002** (first time since Wall era).
- Four interchange "corners": **Gesundbrunnen (N), Ostkreuz (E), Südkreuz (S), Westkreuz (W)** — split the ring into quadrants.
- Frequency: every 10 min base; **every 5 min weekday peak** (~05:00–10:45 and ~14:00–20:45). Night gap on weekdays; Fri/Sat nights every 15 min (~75 min loop).
- Whole Ring sits inside **zone AB** → an AB ticket covers it. Airport (BER) is zone C, NOT on the ring.
- **Fares (Jan 2026):** Single AB **€4.00** (valid 2h, one direction, **no round trips / circular journeys**); Day ticket (24h) AB **€11.20** (unlimited, round trips allowed). Kurzstrecke €2.80; single ABC €5.00.
  - => For a full sightseeing loop back to your start, the correct ticket is a **day ticket**, not a single (a single is not valid for a round trip).

## What each quadrant unlocks (tourist value)
- **N/E (Gesundbrunnen→Ostkreuz):** Prenzlauer Berg edge (Schönhauser Allee, Prenzlauer Allee → Kollwitzkiez, Sunday markets); Mauerpark near Gesundbrunnen/Eberswalder; Friedrichshain via Frankfurter Allee (Karl-Marx-Allee). Ostkreuz = biggest interchange, gateway to RAW/East Side Gallery area.
- **E/S (Ostkreuz→Südkreuz):** Treptower Park (Soviet War Memorial — huge, free), Spree; Sonnenallee/Neukölln (Arab + Turkish food, nightlife); Hermannstraße; Tempelhof → Tempelhofer Feld (former airport field, now park).
- **S/W (Südkreuz→Westkreuz):** Schöneberg; leafy west (Bundesplatz, Heidelberger Platz); Halensee near the west end of Ku'damm.
- **W/N (Westkreuz→Gesundbrunnen):** Messe Nord/ICC (trade fair); Westend; Jungfernheide; Westhafen; Wedding — less touristy, more everyday Berlin.
