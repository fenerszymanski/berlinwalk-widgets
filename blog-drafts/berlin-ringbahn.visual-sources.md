# Berlin Ringbahn — visual sources (internal, not for publish body)

Selection method: Wikimedia Commons API search (train, Ostkreuz, ring viaduct,
Treptower memorial, Tempelhofer Feld, Gesundbrunnen, Südkreuz), 38 candidates
filtered to >=1200px and CC/free licences, contact sheet reviewed at
`blog-drafts/images/berlin-ringbahn/contact-sheet.png`. No AI generation used
for article images. All five are real photos with reusable CC licences, so a
reader-facing Image Credits block is required in the post.

## Final set (all under blog-drafts/images/berlin-ringbahn/optimized/)

1. `01-ringbahn-bridge-s-bahn-cover.jpg` — COVER. Red/ochre S-Bahn crossing the
   curving iron Ringbahn bridge over the Oberspree. Distinctive, instantly reads
   as "the ring", strong at card-crop.
   - Source: Wikimedia Commons, "Berlin Ringbahnbrücke Oberspree lub 2020-11-15"
   - Author: Geoprofi Lars · Licence: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin_Ringbahnbr%C3%BCcke_Oberspree_lub_2020-11-15.jpg

2. `02-s41-ring-departure-board.jpg` — the blue platform display reading
   "S41 · in 2 min · Ring ↻ · Schönhauser Allee · Landsberger Allee · Frankfurter
   Allee · Ostkreuz · Neukölln · Südkreuz". Perfect for the S41/S42 direction
   section (shows the clockwise loop of corner stops).
   - Source: "Berlin S-Bahn Bhf Gesundbrunnen (S41 0614)"
   - Author: Michael.F.H.Barth · Licence: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin_S-Bahn_Bhf_Gesundbrunnen_(S41_0614).jpg

3. `03-suedkreuz-platform-interchange.jpg` — modern red S-Bahn at an airy
   glass-roofed Ring interchange (Südkreuz). Represents a "corner" interchange.
   - Source: "Berlin- Bahnhof Südkreuz- auf Bahnsteig zu Gleis 11 ... 7.8.2010"
   - Author: Jivee Blau · Licence: CC BY-SA 3.0
   - https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin-_Bahnhof_S%C3%BCdkreuz-_auf_Bahnsteig_zu_Gleis_11-_S-Bahn_Berlin_DBAG-Baureihe_481_7.8.2010.jpg

4. `04-treptower-park-soviet-memorial.jpg` — the grand axial view of the Soviet
   War Memorial in Treptower Park. What the Ring's east/south side unlocks.
   - Source: "Berlin - Sowjetisches Ehrenmal im Treptower Park"
   - Author: Marek Śliwecki · Licence: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin_-_Sowjetisches_Ehrenmal_im_Treptower_Park.jpg

5. `05-tempelhofer-feld-runway.jpg` — the former airport runway at
   Tempelhofer Feld with a lone figure and a wide pastel sky. The "former
   airport, now free park" story, summer-relevant.
   - Source: "Berlin Tempelhof Airport Runway 09L - Tempelhofer Feld (15159356029)"
   - Author: Tony Webster · Licence: CC BY 2.0
   - https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin_Tempelhof_Airport_Runway_09L_-_Tempelhofer_Feld_(15159356029).jpg
