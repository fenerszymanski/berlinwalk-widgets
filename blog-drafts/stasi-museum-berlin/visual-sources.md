# Stasi Museum Berlin — Visual Sources

Post slug: `stasi-museum-berlin`. All images are real photographs from Wikimedia
Commons under the stated licenses. No AI visuals used in the article body.
Reader-facing Image Credits block is included at the foot of the post for the
CC BY / CC BY-SA images.

## Final image set (5)

1. **cover-mielke-office.jpg** (COVER + top-of-body embed)
   - Erich Mielke's preserved office suite inside House 1, Stasi Museum.
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:2012-06-Stasimuseum_Berlin_Buero_Erich_Mielke_01_anagoria.JPG
   - Author: Anagoria — License: CC BY 3.0
   - Rationale: instantly reads as a preserved GDR ministerial suite; strong,
     topic-specific, works at card size. Best cover of the set.

2. **stasi-hq-building.jpg** (How to visit)
   - The former Stasi headquarters complex on Ruschestraße / Frankfurter Allee,
     Berlin-Lichtenberg, with the U-Bahn Magdalenenstraße entrance visible.
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Ehemalige-Stasi-Zentrale-Frankfurter-Allee-Ruschestr-Berlin-Lichtenberg.jpg
   - Author: Gunnar Klack — License: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Rationale: shows the real scale of the ministry and helps orientation.

3. **surveillance-gear.jpg** (How the Stasi watched people)
   - GDR-era eavesdropping and tape-recording equipment on museum display.
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Stasi_Surveillance_Equipment_-_DDR_Museum_-_Eastern_Berlin_-_Germany.jpg
   - Author: Adam Jones, Ph.D. — License: CC BY-SA 3.0
   - Rationale: evocative of the surveillance apparatus; supports the widget.
   - Note: exhibit photographed at the DDR Museum; caption kept generic and
     accurate ("Stasi eavesdropping and recording equipment on display").

4. **hohenschoenhausen-cells.jpg** (The prison at Hohenschönhausen)
   - Cell-block corridor, Gedenkstätte Berlin-Hohenschönhausen (Stasi remand prison).
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Cells_course_Berlin-Hohensch%C3%B6nhausen_apel.JPG
   - Author: Denis Apel — License: CC BY-SA 2.0 DE
   - Rationale: strong, atmospheric, unmistakably a Stasi prison corridor.

5. **secretariat.jpg** (Inside the preserved ministry)
   - "Sekretariat / Leiter" anteroom, preserved Stasi headquarters interior.
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Sekretariat-Stasi_headquarters,_Berlin.jpg
   - Author: Roi Boshi — License: Public domain
   - Rationale: second preserved-office view; the "Sekretariat" sign adds
     specificity. PD, so credit optional; included for completeness.
