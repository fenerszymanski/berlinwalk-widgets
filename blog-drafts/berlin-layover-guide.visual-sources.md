## Selection rationale
- Cover (Terminal 1) instantly signals "airport / layover" at card size and is a clean dusk composition.
- S9-at-BER is the single most useful transport image for this post: the S9 runs straight to Alexanderplatz, which is the article's core recommendation.
- Images 03-06 show, in walking order, the historic core a layover traveller would actually see: TV Tower anchor, Museum Island riverside, Unter den Linden, and an empty morning Brandenburg Gate (apt for an early layover).

## Rejected
- 1945 historical Brandenburg Gate photos (off-topic for a practical layover post).
- Night-only Brandenburg Gate shots (a daytime/morning gate fits the layover story better).
