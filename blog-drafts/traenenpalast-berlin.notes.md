## Research Sources

- Haus der Geschichte visitor info, checked 2026-06-25: https://www.hdg.de/en/traenenpalast/visitor-information
  - Free admission.
  - Address: Reichstagufer 17, 10117 Berlin.
  - Regular hours listed as Tue-Fri 9:00-18:00, Sat-Sun 10:00-18:00, Monday closed.
  - Audio guide and access notes.
- Haus der Geschichte permanent exhibition: https://www.hdg.de/en/traenenpalast/permanent-exhibition
  - Friedrichstraße checkpoint, painful farewells, passport/customs control, surveillance, original passport-control booth, personal stories, fall of the Wall.
- Berlin.de attraction page: https://www.berlin.de/en/attractions-and-sights/3560337-3104052-traenenpalast.en.html

  - Permanent exhibition since 2011, original objects/documents/films/interviews, free entry, hours.

  - Used for comparison: free admission, visitor/documentation centre Tue-Sun 10:00-18:00, outdoor exhibition 8:00-22:00.
