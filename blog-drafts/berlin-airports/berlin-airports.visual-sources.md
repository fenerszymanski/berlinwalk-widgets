## Rationale
- Cover: clean, recognizable BER T1 facade at dusk. Reads instantly at card size, topic-specific, editorially composed. Not a random snapshot.
- T2: shows the smaller adjacent low-cost terminal, supports the "one airport, two terminals" section.
- FEX: the practical "how to reach the city" image, the actual Airport Express train.
- Tempelhofer Feld: the strongest single image of "a closed Berlin airport you can still visit" — runway now a park.
- Tegel hexagon: the most iconic closed-airport shape, supports the Tegel section.

## Rejected
- Various interior/gate shots of T1: utilitarian, weak as editorial images.
- Tempelhof portrait full-frame: cropped to landscape for body/gallery use.
- Aerial BER shots: less legible than the facade for a card/cover.
