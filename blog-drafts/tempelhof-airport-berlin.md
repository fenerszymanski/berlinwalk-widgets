## Official Research Sources

- Tempelhofer Feld visitor page: `https://www.tempelhoferfeld.de/en/service-infos/plan-your-visit/`
- Tempelhof Airport guided tours: `https://www.thf-berlin.de/en/your-visit/guided-tours`
- Grün Berlin Tempelhofer Feld page: `https://gruen-berlin.de/en/projects/parks/tempelhofer-feld`

Key facts used:

- Tempelhofer Feld admission is free.
- Opening hours are seasonal; June and July are listed as 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM, August as 6:00 AM to 10:30 PM, December as 7:30 AM to 5:00 PM.
- The official visitor page recommends public transport because parking is very limited.
- Access examples: S41/S42/S47 to Tempelhof, U6 to Tempelhof or Paradestraße, U8 to Leinestraße or Boddinstraße, M43 around Columbiadamm.
- Field rules include paved-area cycling/skating, dog-leash rules outside designated areas, nature protection zones, and designated-area barbecue rules.
- THF guided tours cover parts of the 300,000-square-meter airport building and are usually two hours; public tours take place daily except Tuesdays.
- Grün Berlin describes the field as over 300 hectares and open to all since 2010.
