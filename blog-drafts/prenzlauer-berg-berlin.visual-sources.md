# Prenzlauer Berg Berlin — Visual Sources

All images are genuine Wikimedia Commons photographs of the real Prenzlauer Berg
locations. Optimized copies in `images/prenzlauer-berg-berlin/optimized/`, raw
originals in `images/prenzlauer-berg-berlin/raw/`. No AI-generated visuals used.

## Final selected set (cover + 5 inline)

1. **01-cover-wasserturm-prenzlauer-berg.jpg** — FEATURED/COVER.
   - Source: File:Berlin Prenzlauer Berg Wasserturm 2022.jpg
   - https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin_Prenzlauer_Berg_Wasserturm_2022.jpg
   - Author: Oberlausitzerin64 · Licence: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Rationale: The Wasserturm is the single most recognizable Prenzlauer Berg
     landmark. Portrait original cropped to a landscape cover keeping the spire,
     octagonal top and brick rotunda against summer sky + trees. Strong at card
     crop size, instantly identifiable.

2. **02-eberswalder-konnopke.jpg** — Stop 1 (Eberswalder Straße + Konnopke's).
   - Source: File:Konnopke's Imbiß in Berlin.jpg
   - https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Konnopke%27s_Imbi%C3%9F_in_Berlin.jpg
   - Author: Richard Mischau, Britta Mischau · Licence: CC0
   - Rationale: Yellow U2 train on the green iron viaduct with Konnopke's yellow
     currywurst stand directly underneath — the iconic Eberswalder Straße scene.
     Square original cropped to landscape central band.

3. **03-kulturbrauerei-tower.jpg** — Stop 2 (Kulturbrauerei).
   - Source: File:Berlin 07-2011 (ubt-3).JPG
   - https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin_07-2011_(ubt-3).JPG
   - Author: Tomasz Sienicki · Licence: CC BY 3.0
   - Rationale: Natural (non-HDR) shot of the yellow-brick Kulturbrauerei tower
     clearly labelled "KulturBrauerei", with the Restaurant wing. Rejected a
     heavily HDR-processed alternative (Kulturbrauerei 3814522743) as too
     oversaturated/artificial.

4. **04-kollwitzplatz-market.jpg** — Stop 3 (Kollwitzplatz market).
   - Source: File:Prenzlauer Berg Ökomarkt Kollwitzplatz 2020-001.jpg
   - https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Prenzlauer_Berg_%C3%96komarkt_Kollwitzplatz_2020-001.jpg
   - Author: Fridolin freudenfett · Licence: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Rationale: Golden autumn light, leafy trees, market stalls and beer-bench
     seating on the square — warm and lively. Rejected a wintry/grey Kollwitz
     market shot (Wochenmarkt Kollwitzplatz-002) as dull for a summer guide.

5. **05-kastanienallee.jpg** — Stop 5 (Kastanienallee).
   - Source: File:Kastanienallee, Berlin (IMG 20140504 174718 nopm ).jpg
   - https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Kastanienallee,_Berlin_(IMG_20140504_174718_nopm_).jpg
   - Author: Matti Blume · Licence: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Rationale: Leafy Kastanienallee street with cafés and Altbau facades — the
     café-street character of the district. Kept portrait (cropped bottom).

6. **06-mauerpark-flea-market.jpg** — Mauerpark section.
   - Source: File:Flohmarkt im Mauerpark, Berlin, Germany (Unsplash).jpg
   - https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Flohmarkt_im_Mauerpark,_Berlin,_Germany_(Unsplash).jpg
   - Author: Roman Kraft · Licence: CC0
   - Rationale: Box of old vintage photographs at a Mauerpark flea-market stall —
     evokes the Sunday flea-market treasure-hunt. Rejected a quiet/grey empty
     Mauerpark parkland shot as missing the market energy.

## Rejected

- Berliner Wasserturm Knaackstraße.jpg (CC BY-SA 4.0) — black-and-white GDR-era
  political rally in front of the tower. Event-specific and misleading for a
  modern district guide. Rejected.
- Wochenmarkt Kollwitzplatz-002.jpg — wintry/grey, cluttered "XXL Bockwurst"
  signage. Rejected in favour of the golden-light Ökomarkt shot.
- Kulturbrauerei (3814522743).jpg — heavy HDR oversaturation. Rejected.
- 20170423 Maurpark 4773.jpg — quiet, overcast empty parkland; misses the
  famous Sunday flea-market/karaoke energy. Rejected.
