## Verified independently by me (primary sources, 2026-07-20)

1. **Fastest Berlin -> Dresden rail journey: 1 h 39 min. Up to 11 direct
   connections per day. Cheapest advance fare shown "ab 14,99 EUR".**
   Source: https://www.bahn.de/reisen/view/verbindung/berlin/dresden.shtml
   (DB's own route page; sample connections rendered as RJ/RJX, i.e. the
   Berlin–Prague Railjet, not the old EC branding — do not write "EC" without
   rechecking.)

2. **Berlin Stadtbahn closed to regional and long-distance trains between
   Charlottenburg and Ostbahnhof, 14 June – 12 December 2026.** S-Bahn keeps
   running on the Stadtbahn as normal (except three weekends). So Alexanderplatz,
   Friedrichstraße, Zoologischer Garten and Ostbahnhof are NOT departure stations
   for this trip right now.
   Sources: https://www.vbb.de/unterwegs-im-vbb/baustellen-bei-regional-und-s-bahn/stadtbahnsperrung-2026/
   https://viz.berlin.de/aktuelle-meldungen/sperrung-der-berliner-stadtbahn-14.06-12.12.26/
   https://www.dbregio-berlin-brandenburg.de/db-regio-no/Fahren/Baustellen-und-Stoerungen/stadtbahnsperrung
   NOTE: Berlin Hbf has two levels. The Dresden trains use the deep north–south
   level via Südkreuz, which is not the closed Stadtbahn level. Confirm before
   writing that Berlin Hbf still works.

3. **Dresden Hauptbahnhof Kreuzungsbauwerk works, 9 February – mid-November 2026.**
   Affects the Saxony–Franconia axis, not the Berlin approach: S3, RE 3
   (Dresden–Hof) and RB 30 (Dresden–Zwickau) do not serve Dresden Hbf; from
   1 May to mid-November the two IC 17 Chemnitz train pairs run from
   Dresden-Neustadt and the Dresden Hbf stop is dropped. Replacement bus runs
   Dresden Hbf (Strehlener Str.) – Nürnberger Platz – Dresden-Plauen, Mon–Fri
   06:00–20:00 and Sat/Sun/holidays 08:00–20:00, every 10 minutes.
   Sources: https://www.deutschebahn.com/de/presse/presse-regional/pr-leipzig-de/aktuell/presseinformationen/Halbzeit-am-Kreuzungsbauwerk-Dresden-Hauptbahnhof-13740868
   https://www.mitteldeutsche-regiobahn.de/de/strecken/fahrplanabweichungen/baustelle-dresden-2026

4. **Deutschlandticket / VBB regional tickets are no longer accepted on DB
   long-distance trains on the Berlin – Doberlug-Kirchhain – Elsterwerda
   section since the December 2025 timetable change.** The old
   "Deutschlandticket on the IC to Elsterwerda" trick is dead.
   Source: https://dresden.t-online.de/region/dresden/id_100957210/deutsche-bahn-deutschlandticket-gilt-bald-nicht-mehr-im-ic-nach-berlin.html
   RECHECK: find a primary DB/VBB source before publishing.

5. **SKD (Zwinger, Gemäldegalerie Alte Meister) closed Mondays; Zwinger ticket
   16 EUR / 12 EUR reduced.**
   Source: https://gemaeldegalerie.skd.museum/en/visit/
   https://www.der-dresdner-zwinger.de/en/dresden-stable-yard/opening-hours-and-ticket-rates/

6. **Frauenkirche interior is free to enter**, open for visitation roughly
   10:00–11:30 and 13:00–17:30 daily. Dome ascent is paid; exact price/hours not
   captured from the official site yet.
   Source: https://www.frauenkirche-dresden.de/en/
