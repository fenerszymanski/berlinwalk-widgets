## Rationale / rejections

- Cover: chose the Elbphilharmonie (Mücke) over a Speicherstadt cover because it is the
  single most recognisable modern Hamburg icon, has a dramatic cloud sky and water
  foreground, and its wide 16:9 crop reads well as a Wix card and social image.
- Speicherstadt (Fred Romero) moved to inline harbour-section use. Its sky is a flat
  overcast white, fine as a supporting canal shot but weaker than the Elbphilharmonie for
  a hero. Preferred over the CC0 Meduana Speicherstadt so every article image carries an
  attribution credit and the credit count stays consistent.
- Alster + Rathaus (Thomas Wolf): a curated Commons Quality Image; one frame ties the town
  hall, both lakes and the old-town panorama together, covering the "other Hamburg" section
  without a second image.
- ICE at Hamburg Hbf (Wikiolo): the departure board literally reads Berlin-Spandau /
  Berlin Hbf, which reinforces the getting-there section perfectly.
- Rejected: Reeperbahn daytime (Dguendel) — cluttered winter intersection with bare trees,
  parked cars and dated advertising; dull and off-tone, so the article uses no Reeperbahn
  image and treats it only as a "skip by day" note. Dedicated Rathaus façade (Rabich) —
  redundant with the Alster panorama, and its fussy required credit format was avoidable.
- Contact sheet: the 6 downloaded candidates were each viewed at full optimized size before
  selection; the Reeperbahn candidate was dropped on quality grounds.
