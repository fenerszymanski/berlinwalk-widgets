are gitignored; every raw can be rebuilt from the source URL below.

Optimisation: every master resized to 1600px on the long edge at JPEG quality
86 before upload to Wix Media, per the 2026-07-19 rule.

### 1. cover-hauptbahnhof.jpg — FEATURED / COVER

- Page: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:2018-08-08_DE_Berlin-Mitte,_Berlin_Hauptbahnhof_(49814284187).jpg

## Rejected

- **Zoologischer Garten** (`File:Berlin dworzec Zoologischer Garten 1.jpg`,
  Andrzej Otrębski, CC BY-SA 4.0). Downloaded, optimised and viewed, then cut on
  the quality gate: flat grey sky, the bottom third is taxi rank and cobbles,
  and a McDonald's sign sits mid-frame. Legally fine, visually dull. The post is
  better with six strong images than seven uneven ones. Zoo is still covered in
  the body text and inside the widget.
- **Berlin Gesundbrunnen** — no strong image exists on Commons. The best
  candidates are phone HDR frames or a flat blue-sky record shot. Deliberately
  left without a photo rather than padding with a weak one. Open task: Yusuf
  shooting Gesundbrunnen's platform hall himself would fix this permanently.
- **Berlin Südkreuz** — usable images exist, but the station is a one-line
  mention in the body and the article did not need a seventh image.
- Panorama stitches of Hauptbahnhof (extreme aspect ratios, crop badly),
  FAL-licensed Ostbahnhof/Charlottenburg shots (awkward licence), and all
  sub-1200px results.
