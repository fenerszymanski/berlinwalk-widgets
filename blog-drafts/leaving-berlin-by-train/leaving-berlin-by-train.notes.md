## Verified facts and sources

### 1. Stadtbahn closure

- 14 June to 12 December 2026, section Berlin-Charlottenburg to Berlin
  Ostbahnhof, closed to regional and long-distance trains.
  https://www.vbb.de/unterwegs-im-vbb/baustellen-bei-regional-und-s-bahn/stadtbahnsperrung-2026/
  https://viz.berlin.de/aktuelle-meldungen/sperrung-der-berliner-stadtbahn-14.06-12.12.26/
- The S-Bahn keeps running the Stadtbahn normally apart from a small number of
  construction weekends with rail replacement buses between Friedrichstrasse and
  Tiergarten. NOTE: viz.berlin lists 26-28 Jun, 24-26 Jul, 31 Jul-2 Aug;
  `PROJECT_MEMORY.md` records 26-29 Jun, 24-27 Jul, 31 Jul-3 Aug. The two
  disagree, so **exact weekend dates are deliberately not published**. The post
  says "a handful of weekends" and links to VBB.
- Most long-distance trains stop in the lower level of Hauptbahnhof
  (`Hauptbahnhof tief`). Replacement stops for Ostbahnhof and Zoologischer
  Garten: Südkreuz, Gesundbrunnen, Ostkreuz, Charlottenburg, Lichtenberg.
- ICE Berlin-Düsseldorf/Köln: from/to Südkreuz via Hauptbahnhof (tief).
- ICE to North Rhine-Westphalia and Frankfurt am Main: depart Charlottenburg
  from 14 June.
- EC Berlin-Poland: via Gesundbrunnen and Lichtenberg from 25 August.
- IC Dresden-Berlin-Rostock: diverted via Ostkreuz.
- Hamburg trains cannot stop at Spandau during the closure.
- EC Hamburg-Prague trains start and end in Berlin, so Dresden/Prague travellers
  must change in Berlin.
- Regional: no RE/RB trains at all between Charlottenburg and Ostbahnhof.
  RE1 and RE7 start/end at Charlottenburg or Ostbahnhof. RE2 diverted via
  Gesundbrunnen, Ostkreuz and Schöneweide. RB10 daytime services cancelled until
  October (night service 00:00-04:00 Hauptbahnhof-Nauen only). RB14 terminates
  Charlottenburg. RB21 serves Charlottenburg instead of Gesundbrunnen. RB23
  partly suspended.
  https://www.dbregio-berlin-brandenburg.de/db-regio-no/Fahren/Baustellen-und-Stoerungen/stadtbahnsperrung

### 2. DB long-distance fares

Source: bahn.de official pages, checked 2026-07-21.
https://www.bahn.de/angebot/spar-flexpreis
https://www.bahn.de/faq/was-ist-der-unterschied-zwischen-sparpreis-und-flexpreis

- Super Sparpreis: from EUR 6.99. Bound to the exact long-distance train on the
  ticket. `Umbuchung, Umtausch und Stornierung sind ausgeschlossen.`
- Sparpreis: from EUR 21.99. Bound to the exact train. Change or cancel until
  one day before the first day of validity for a EUR 10 fee.
- Flexpreis: any train on that route on the booked day, stopovers allowed.
  City-Ticket for local transport in participating cities is included. Free
  change/cancel until one day before the first day of validity; EUR 30 from the
  first day of validity.
- City-Ticket must be bought as an add-on with Super Sparpreis and Sparpreis.

### 3. Deutschlandticket

- EUR 63 per month since January 2026 (was EUR 58).
- Valid on RE, RB, IRE and all local transport (S-Bahn, U-Bahn, tram, bus).
- Not valid on ICE, IC, EC or FlixTrain.
- **Berlin-Brandenburg change:** from the 14 December 2025 timetable change, DB
  Fernverkehr ended its contract with VBB, so VBB tickets and the
  Deutschlandticket are no longer accepted on any DB long-distance train in the
  region. This killed the Berlin-Elsterwerda / Doberlug-Kirchhain IC, the
  Potsdam-Berlin-Cottbus IC and the Berlin-Prenzlau ICE shortcuts. Most English
  guides still recommend these.
  https://www.deutschebahn.com/de/presse/presse-regional/pr-berlin-de/aktuell/presseinformationen/Fahrplan-2026-alle-Neuerungen-fuer-Berlin-Brandenburg-und-Mecklenburg-Vorpommern-im-Ueberblick-13565034
  https://berlin.t-online.de/region/berlin/id_100629424/berlin-brandenburg-deutschlandticket-gilt-ab-2026-in-ic-und-ice-nicht-mehr.html
- Replacement for Elsterwerda: RE8, roughly 1h30 to Berlin Hbf after the
  Dresdner Bahn opened.
