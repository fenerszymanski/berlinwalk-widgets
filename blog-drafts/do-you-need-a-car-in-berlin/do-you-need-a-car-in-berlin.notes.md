## Verified facts (2026-07-18 WebSearch)
- Umweltzone = everything inside the S-Bahn ring; green Umweltplakette required; no tourist/rental exemption; since 2025 only green sticker permitted; missing sticker = 105 EUR fine; order online ~6 EUR / in person 5 EUR. German rentals and car-share cars almost always carry it.
- Parking: central street ~1-3 EUR/hr; garages ~2-4 EUR/hr, daily cap near 24 EUR; district-by-district Parkraumbewirtschaftung; Handyparken apps bill by the minute.
- Car-sharing: free-floating Miles, SHARE NOW, Sixt share; station-based Cambio; ~6,000 vehicles across ~a dozen operators. WeShare was integrated into Miles (not named as active). Sources: visitBerlin, berlin.de.
- Day trips: Potsdam/Sanssouci and Sachsenhausen are easier by train; a car helps mainly off the rail network (Brandenburg lakes, Spreewald, Baltic).
