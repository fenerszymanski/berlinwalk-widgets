downloaded, licensed, and credited in their original folders). They are Wikimedia
Commons files under CC licenses that require attribution, so the post carries a
reader-facing Image Credits block. Selected for a "how much of the city fits in
your trip" narrative: a wide skyline for the whole city, the walkable historic
core for day 1, Museum Island for the museum day, Tiergarten for the slower
extra day, and Sanssouci for the day-trip day.

## Cover rationale

`cover-berlin-skyline-how-many-days.jpg` is a clear, warm, high-angle skyline
with the TV Tower rising over Mitte. At listing-card size it reads instantly as
"all of Berlin," which is exactly the decision the post is about: how much of
this can you realistically fit into your trip.

## Reader-facing Image Credits

Four of the five images use CC BY / CC BY-SA licenses that require attribution.
The Tiergarten photo is CC0 (no attribution required) but is credited anyway for
consistency. The public post includes an Image Credits block listing author and
license for each image, via Wikimedia Commons.

## Rejected / not used

- Portrait crops and duller storefront/street-corner shots from the same folders
  were skipped in favour of clean, wide, instantly readable views.
- No AI-generated article visuals were used; all five are real licensed photos.
