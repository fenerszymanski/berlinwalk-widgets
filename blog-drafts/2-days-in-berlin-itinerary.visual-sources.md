## Editorial decision

- The Museum Island cover gives an immediate central-Berlin and route-planning signal at card crop size.
- Brandenburg Gate is quiet, legible and not dominated by a crowd.
- Bernauer Strasse shows the border system, not a generic Wall fragment.
- East Side Gallery includes visitors for walking scale and stays exact to the route.
- Oberbaum Bridge is the correct final-evening handover into Kreuzberg.

## Rejected

- Generic skyline images without route context.
- Crowded selfie images at Brandenburg Gate.
- Archival Wall scenes that could be mistaken for the current visitor route.
