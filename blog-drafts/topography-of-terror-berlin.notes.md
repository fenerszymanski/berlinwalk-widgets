## Source Notes

- Official Topography directions/opening hours:
  `https://www.topographie.de/en/your-visit/directions-opening-hours`
  - Exhibitions daily 10:00-20:00.
  - Outdoor areas until nightfall, 20:00 at the latest.
  - Free admission.
  - Address: Niederkirchnerstraße 8, 10963 Berlin.
  - Public transport: U-Bahn Potsdamer Platz or Kochstraße; S-Bahn Anhalter Bahnhof or Potsdamer Platz.
- Official permanent exhibition:
  `https://www.topographie.de/en/exhibitions/topography-of-terror`
  - Focuses on Gestapo, SS and Reich Security Main Office on Wilhelmstraße and Prinz-Albrecht-Straße.
  - Admission free, duration approx. 60 minutes, German/English.
- Official outdoor exhibition:
  `https://www.topographie.de/en/exhibitions/berlin-1933-1945`
  - `Berlin 1933-1945. Between Propaganda and Terror`; admission free, German/English.
- Official site tour:
  `https://www.topographie.de/en/exhibitions/site-tour`
  - Site tour in 15 stations, admission free, German/English.
  - Oriented around material traces of the site, including marked ground memorial and exposed foundations.
- Official audio guide:
  `https://www.topographie.de/en/your-visit/audio-guide`
  - Approx. 60 minutes.
  - Device can be borrowed free at service desk.
  - Can also be used through free Wi-Fi on own smartphone; bring headphones.
  - Page lists 22 languages.
- visitBerlin confirmation:
  `https://www.visitberlin.de/en/topography-terror`
  - Daily opening 10:00-20:00.
  - Site between 1933 and 1945 held principal instruments of Nazi persecution and terror: Gestapo, SS high command/security service and Reich Security Main Office.
- Berlin.de transport fares:
  `https://www.berlin.de/en/public-transportation/1772016-2913840-tickets-fares-and-route-maps.en.html`
  - 2026 AB single ticket EUR 4.00, AB 24-hour ticket EUR 11.20.
- Memorial to the Murdered Jews of Europe:
  `https://www.stiftung-denkmal.de/en/memorials/memorial-to-the-murdered-jews-of-europe/`
  - Admission free; field open 24 hours; Information Centre has separate opening/closure notes.
- Berlin Wall Memorial visitor information:
  `https://www.stiftung-berliner-mauer.de/en/berlin-wall-memorial/visit/information`
  - Admission free; visitor center/documentation center opening context; used for comparison only.
