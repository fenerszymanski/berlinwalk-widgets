## Verified facts (checked 2026-07-22)

Prices (official https://www.tropical-islands.de/en/preise-oeffnungszeiten/preise
and German /preise-oeffnungszeiten/preise):
- Day ticket "Pure Tropics": online from EUR 34.90 adult (18+), from EUR 29.90
  teen (13-17), from EUR 29.90 child (4-12); on-site regular EUR 55.90 adult /
  48.50 teen / 45.50 child; high-season EUR 59.90 adult; special days EUR 69 adult.
  Prices are date-based; online is cheaper than walk-in.
- Children up to 3 ("Kinder bis 3 Jahre"): free.
- Included: Tropical World + outdoor area AMAZONIA, 08:00-23:30 (day guests;
  the resort is NOT 24/7 for day visitors).
- Sauna & Spa add-on: +EUR 14 adult (+8 reduced). "Tropics & Sauna" combined day
  ticket online from EUR 39.90 adult.
- Evening ticket 18:00-23:30: online from EUR 29.90 adult / 24.90 teen / 22.90 child.
- Family ticket: 2 adults + 3 children (4-12) from EUR 129.90; single parent + 3
  children from EUR 119.90.
- Reduced = students, 65+, disabled 50%+, volunteers.
- Sauna landscape is textile-free (German custom), ~10,000 m2, Europe's largest
  tropical sauna complex (opened 2007): official sauna page
  https://www.tropical-islands.de/en/entdecke-die-tropen/unsere-attraktionen/sauna-und-spa

Getting there (official /en/planen-sie-ihren-besuch/relevante-informationen/anfahrt):
- Station: Brand Tropical Islands (renamed 2015; Berlin-Goerlitz line).
- Free shuttle bus station->dome, timed to trains, does NOT wait for delays;
  official timetable PDF linked from the site.
- Car: A13 exit Staakow, ~50 km south of Berlin-Schoenefeld, +3 km signed.
- Parking: P1 EUR 8 / 24h (near dome), P2/P3 EUR 6. Address:
  Tropical-Islands-Allee 1, 15910 Krausnick.
- Trains: RE2 hourly (toward Cottbus); RB24 also serves Brand (hourly). ~44-45
  min from Ostkreuz (7 stops; berlintraveltips corroboration; do not print an
  exact minute count harder than "about 45 minutes").
- 2026 CRITICAL (aligned with published leaving-berlin-by-train durable facts):
  Stadtbahn closed to regional/long-distance trains 14 Jun - 12 Dec 2026
  (Charlottenburg-Ostbahnhof). RE2 diverted via Gesundbrunnen, Ostkreuz,
  Schoeneweide. So board at OSTKREUZ (S3/S5/S7/S9 connect), not Hauptbahnhof
  Stadtbahn/Alexanderplatz/Friedrichstrasse. S-Bahn itself unaffected apart from
  a handful of construction weekends (dates disputed between sources; do NOT
  print exact weekend dates — link VBB instead; prior editorial decision).
  RE2 major works window 14 Jun-12 Dec 2026; a full Ostkreuz-Halbe closure ran
  10-18 Jul 2026 (ended). Article advises checking VBB Fahrinfo on the day.
- Fares: Deutschlandticket EUR 63/month (Jan 2026) valid — Brand is inside VBB,
  regional trains only. Brandenburg-Berlin-Ticket EUR 36.50 (2026) up to 5
  people, weekdays valid from 09:00 (weekends from 00:00) — bahndampf.de +
  VBB 2026 fare-change reporting. Berlin ABC ticket is NOT enough (Brand is
  beyond zone C). VBB single Berlin->Brand exists (~EUR 9.50 per third-party;
  NOT printed as exact fact — article says check VBB app).

Hall/history (Wikipedia Tropical_Islands_Resort + CargoLifter + Brand-Briesen Airfield):
- Aerium: 360 m long, 210 m wide, 107 m high, largest free-standing hall in the
  world; built 2000 by CargoLifter AG (founded 1996) for airship production on
  the former Soviet Brand-Briesen airfield; CargoLifter insolvent 2002; hall
  sold to Malaysian Tanjong group for EUR 17.5M; resort opened 19 Dec 2004;
  owned by Parques Reunidos since Dec 2018 (EUR 226M).
- Inside: 26 C air, ~64% humidity; rainforest ~50,000 plants / 600 species;
  Tropical Sea: 140 m pool, 4,400 m2, 200 m sandy beach; Bali Lagoon 1,200 m2;
  waterslide tower 27 m; AMAZONIA outdoor ~35,000 m2 (two pools, sports).
- Overnight: rooms, lodges, tents inside/around the hall (no prices printed).
