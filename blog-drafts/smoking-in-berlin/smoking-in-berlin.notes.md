## Verified facts (WebSearch, 2026-07-18) with sources

**Indoor smoking (Berlin exception)**
- Federal + Berlin Nichtraucherschutzgesetz: smoking banned in enclosed restaurants/workplaces since 2008.
- Berlin exception: single-room bar under 75 m², no hot food served, no under-18s admitted, may allow smoking as a marked Raucherkneipe. Larger/food venues must be smoke-free.
- Fines: smoker up to ~100 EUR; bar owner ~1,000 EUR. Enforcement historically loose, Mitte + Friedrichshain-Kreuzberg increasing checks.
- Sources: en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Smoking_in_Germany ; pro-rauchfrei.de state overview ; iamexpat.de "Berlin authorities crack down on smoking in bars".

**Vaping / e-cigarettes**
- Treated the same as tobacco under the protection act and transport ban.
- Source: german-way.com smoking-and-vaping ; tobaccocontrollaws.org Germany e-cigarettes.

**Public transport**
- Smoking + vaping banned on all trains, trams, buses, U-Bahn, S-Bahn and platforms; only marked smoking areas on some open-air platforms are exempt; underground platforms fully smoke-free. S-Bahn Hausordnung explicitly includes e-cigs.
- On-the-spot platform penalty ~40 EUR (typical).
- Source: sbahn.berlin rules-of-conduct ; thevapersguide.com Germany.

**Cannabis (CanG / KCanG, in force since 1 April 2024, still in force 2026)**
- Adults 18+ may possess up to 25 g in public, 50 g at home, grow up to 3 plants. 18-21 limited to <10% THC from clubs.
- Public consumption legal EXCEPT: within 100 m (sight range) of schools, playgrounds, daycare, sports grounds; in pedestrian zones 07:00-20:00; around minors; in/on public transport; while driving.
- Buying: no shops. Only non-profit cannabis social clubs, require ~6 months German residence -> tourists cannot legally buy. Street dealing still illegal.
- Commercial retail (pillar 2) not enacted; under political discussion in 2026 (fluid after federal elections). ~366 clubs approved by March 2026.
- Sources: bundesgesundheitsministerium.de Cannabis Act FAQ (BMG, official) ; en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cannabis_Act_(Germany) ; cannabisgermany.org overview ; iamexpat.de "Buying cannabis in Germany: What are the rules?".
