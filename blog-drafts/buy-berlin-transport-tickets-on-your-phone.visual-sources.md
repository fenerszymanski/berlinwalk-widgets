# Visual sources — buy-berlin-transport-tickets-on-your-phone

All five images are genuine Berlin transit photos from Wikimedia Commons. No AI
generation was used for this post (no free/built-in generator available this run;
paid image APIs are barred for the daily automation). Every image was visually
QA'd on a contact sheet before selection.

Reader-facing attribution: a compact **Image credits** block is included at the
end of the article body because four of the five files are CC BY-SA and require
a credit line. The CC0 file needs no credit but is listed for completeness.

## Selected (5)

1. `01-cover-s-bahn-hauptbahnhof.jpg` — **COVER**. Red-and-cream S-Bahn in motion
   at Berlin Hauptbahnhof, station sign legible. Iconic, instantly Berlin, strong
   at card-crop size.
   - Source: Wikimedia Commons — https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=36865488
   - Author: Martin Falbisoner · License: CC BY-SA 4.0

2. `02-bvg-ticket-machine.jpg` — BVG/tram ticket machine ("Fahrausweise"). Used
   inline for "the old way you can now skip on your phone."
   - Source: Wikimedia Commons — https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=147522299
   - Author: Nicbou · License: CC0 (no attribution required)

3. `03-u-bahn-platform.jpg` — U-Bahn platform at Kaiserin-Augusta-Straße (U6),
   real riders waiting. Inline for the "on the platform, which ticket" moment.
   - Source: Wikimedia Commons — https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=5094424
   - Author: Jcornelius · License: CC BY-SA 3.0 (de)

4. `04-u-bahn-alexanderplatz.jpg` — Yellow U-Bahn train at Alexanderplatz (U5),
   green columns and a live departure board. Inline for fare zones / where to tap.
   - Source: Wikimedia Commons — https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=98175126
   - Author: Geoprofi Lars · License: CC BY-SA 4.0

5. `05-s-bahn-ostkreuz.jpg` — Modern S-Bahn platform at Ostkreuz with a red

   - Source: Wikimedia Commons — https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=122610997
   - Author: Michael.F.H.Barth · License: CC BY-SA 4.0

## Rejected

- `Validator in the bus BVG Berlin (26822543832).jpg` (CC BY 2.0) — **REJECTED**
  as misleading. Despite the "BVG Berlin" Commons title, the photo clearly shows
  Warsaw destinations (Wilanów, Muranów) and Polish text; it is a Warsaw tram
  validator, not Berlin. Replaced by the Alexanderplatz U-Bahn image.
- Generic non-Berlin "phone at subway" stock — rejected as misleading for a
  Berlin-specific post. The phone/app concept is carried by the in-article
  widget instead.
