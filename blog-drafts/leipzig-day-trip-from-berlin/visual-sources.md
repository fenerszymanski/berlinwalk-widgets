## Rejected candidates (why)

- `Völkerschlachtdenkmal C-M.jpg` — visually strong, but the author adds a
  "contact me for commercial use" note on top of CC BY-SA. Contested terms,
  rejected on policy.
- `J23 656 Bf Leipzig Hbf, Bahnsteig 10-11.jpg` — yellow construction barriers
  clutter the platform.
- `20200912 Mädlerpassage 03.jpg` — dark ceiling-dome abstract, unclear at
  card size.
- `Mädlerpassage Leipzig Eingang.jpg` — 600px, too small, dark archway.
- `Thomaskirche leipzig bach.jpg` / `Johann Sebastian Bach - Leipzig.jpg` —
  576px / 680px, below the master bar; replaced with the 4924px CC0 2026 shot.
- `Runde-Ecke-Leipzig.jpg` — utilitarian winter frame with parked cars, weak
  as an editorial image; the building is described in text instead.
- `Exterior of Thomaskirche (Leipzig) 2025.jpg` — parked cars across the base
  of the frame; Westseite 2013 is cleaner.
- Bundesarchiv / Fotothek archival frames — licence-fine but grey and
  documentation-like next to the selected set.
