# Teufelsberg Berlin - Visual Sources

Post: `teufelsberg-berlin` (Tourist Tips + Berlin History)
Selected 2026-07-13. All images legally usable; three require reader-facing
attribution (CC BY-SA / CC BY), two are CC0 (no attribution required but still
credited internally here).

## Selected set (cover + 4 inline)

### 01 - Cover (featured only, not embedded at top of body)
- File: `optimized/01-teufelsberg-berlin-cover.jpg` (1600x1200)
- Raw: `raw/r01-cover.jpg`
- Source: Wikimedia Commons, "Former Teufelsberg Listening Station.jpg"
  https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Former_Teufelsberg_Listening_Station.jpg
- Author: Sarahmirk. License: CC BY-SA 4.0.
- Why: Single frame that tells the whole story at card size: the white radar
  domes and tower on top, plus the colourful street-art murals on the station
  buildings. Instantly readable as Teufelsberg. Strong editorial cover.

### 02 - Radome close-up (Cold War / spy-station section)
- File: `optimized/02-teufelsberg-radomes.jpg` (1600x1066)
- Raw: `raw/r02-radomes.jpg`
- Source: Wikimedia Commons, "Radome Teufelsberg Berlin.jpg"
  https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Radome_Teufelsberg_Berlin.jpg
- Author: RealPixelStreet. License: CC BY-SA 4.0.
- Why: Dramatic monochrome close-up of a torn radome and its geodesic skin.
  Reads as "abandoned Cold War listening post" better than a clean tourist shot.

### 03 - Yellow U-Bahn "TEUFELSBERG" mural (street-art / today section)
- File: `optimized/03-teufelsberg-train-mural.jpg` (1400x1050)
- Raw: `raw/r03-train-mural.jpg`
- Source: Wikimedia Commons, "Teufelsberg listening station graffiti.jpg"
  https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Teufelsberg_listening_station_graffiti.jpg
- Author: Sarahmirk. License: CC BY-SA 4.0.
- Why: A yellow Berlin U-Bahn train mural labelled "TEUFELSBERG". Colourful,
  on-brand yellow, and literally names the place. Represents the site's current
  life as an open-air street-art gallery.

### 04 - Graffiti dome interior (street-art / worth-it section)
- File: `optimized/04-teufelsberg-dome-interior.jpg` (933x1400)
- Raw: `raw/r04-dome-interior.jpg`
- Source: Wikimedia Commons, "Graffiti dome (Unsplash).jpg"
  https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Graffiti_dome_(Unsplash).jpg
- Author: Samuel Zeller. License: CC0 (public domain, no attribution required).
- Why: Looking up inside one of the geodesic radomes, its white panels covered
  in tags. The most recognisable "inside the dome" view.

### 05 - Grunewald forest approach (getting-there section)
- File: `optimized/05-teufelsberg-forest-path.jpg` (1600x1067)
- Raw: `raw/r05-forest-path.jpg`
- Source: Wikimedia Commons, "Teufelsberg Berlin 2021-11-22 02.jpg"
  https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Teufelsberg_Berlin_2021-11-22_02.jpg
- Author: Leonhard Lenz. License: CC0 (public domain, no attribution required).
- Why: The wooded Grunewald slope you actually walk up from the S-Bahn. Supports
  the "getting here is a real trip, plan a half day" advice.

## Rejected / not used
- Leonhard Lenz 2021 series (most frames): repetitive misty forest-path shots;
  domes only appear small and foggy. Used one representative frame only.
- "Teufelsberg at night.jpg": moody but too dark for an inline explainer image.
- "Berlin Teufelsberg von Humboldthoehe": distant hazy view of the wooded hill;
  visually weak, hard to read as Teufelsberg.
- Various 1992 archival domes: low resolution / washed out.
