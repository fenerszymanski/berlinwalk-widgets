# Festival of Lights Berlin 2026 Visual Sources

Status: Yusuf-approved normal night landmark image set. Uploaded to the Wix draft on 2026-06-05.

Because Festival of Lights projections are temporary light-art works, the official FAQ notes that commercial use of photographs of the installations needs permission. This set avoids close-up copyrighted light-art projection photos and uses normal night landmark imagery instead.
