# Festival of Lights Berlin 2026 Visual Candidates

Status: candidate images only. Not uploaded to Wix yet.

Because Festival of Lights projections are temporary light-art works, the official FAQ notes that commercial use of photographs of the installations needs permission. For the first approval pass, this set avoids close-up copyrighted light-art projection photos and uses normal night landmark imagery instead.
