# Festival of Lights Berlin 2026 — AI Image Prompts

Generate these in Yusuf's ChatGPT Pro (or other image tool). Zero API spend.
After generating, drop the files into this folder and Claude/Codex will crop, compress,
brand, upload to Wix, and wire them into the draft, then update `visual-sources.md`.

## Why AI (copyright)

Real Festival of Lights projections are temporary light-art works. Germany's Freedom of
Panorama does not cover temporary projections, and the official FAQ says commercial use of
projection photos needs consent. AI-generated, original festival-style scenes avoid that
problem entirely. Rules baked into every prompt below: original/abstract light patterns
(never a copy of a specific real artwork), realistic recognisable architecture, and no text,
letters, signs, watermarks, or logos.

## General settings

- Style: photorealistic editorial travel photography, cinematic, long-exposure night mood.
- Cover: 16:9, generate as large as possible (need at least 1600 px wide).
- Inline images: 3:2 landscape.
- Avoid: garish/cartoon look, distorted landmarks, gibberish text, visible faces in sharp focus, watermarks.
