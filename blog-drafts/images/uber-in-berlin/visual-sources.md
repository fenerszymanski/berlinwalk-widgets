## Facts verified on 2026-07-10 (Europe/Berlin)
- Uber operates in Germany only as a *Mietwagen* (hire-car) service under the
  Passenger Transport Act (PBefG §49), not as a taxi. No street hails, no taxi
  ranks; the app dispatches licensed private-hire cars (or, via "Uber Taxi",
  real taxis). Sources: originalberlintours.com legal guide 2025,
  welcomepickups.com Berlin Uber guide, taxi-heute.de PBefG explainer.
- Return-to-base rule (§49(4) PBefG / Rückkehrpflicht): private-hire cars must
  return to their operating base after each ride and may not cruise for fares
  like taxis. This is why UberX/Bolt supply is thinner and pickups can be slower,
  especially outside the centre. Source: taxi-heute.de, originalberlintours.com.
- Uber shows a fixed price before you book; it is not a running meter. Berlin
  taxis use a regulated meter: ~EUR 4.30 base then per-km rates. Source:
  welcomepickups.com, berlin.de taxi tariff.
- FreeNow (formerly mytaxi) mainly dispatches licensed Berlin taxis through an
  app, so its price tracks the regulated meter with app payment and tracking.
  Bolt competes on price and is often cheapest, but availability is more
  variable. Sources: berlincitytransfer.de 2026 guide, welcomepickups.com.
- Uber, Bolt and FreeNow all serve BER Airport. The Ride App Pick Up zone is at
  Terminal 1 ground level; the app shows the exact meeting spot after you book.
  Sources: uber.com BER page, berlincitytransfer.de, tripadvisor Berlin forum.
- Rough costs (2026): short in-city hop ~EUR 10-15 for ~5 km; BER Airport to
  central Berlin roughly EUR 50-80 by app depending on surge, versus ~EUR 55-70
  by metered taxi; the Airport Express (FEX)/S-Bahn/regional train is a few euros
  on a normal BVG/VBB ticket. Sources: berlincitytransfer.de,
  berlintourism.org, berlin.de.
- Tipping: rounding up ~5-10% is normal and appreciated for taxis and app rides;
  it is optional, not compulsory. Source: berlin.de etiquette, welcomepickups.com.

Rough prices are given as ranges and framed as estimates in the article, never as
guaranteed fares, because app pricing is dynamic.
