## Facts verified on 2026-07-10
- Flea market: Sundays, 10:00–18:00 (early sellers set up from ~09:00), free entry. Address Bernauer Straße 63, 10435 Berlin. Source: berlin.de, visitBerlin.
- Bearpit Karaoke: Joe Hatchiban's amphitheatre karaoke, Sundays from ~15:00 in good weather, roughly April–October, cancelled in rain/winter. Source: visitBerlin, berlin.de, Time Out.
- Transport: Tram M10 / 12 (Friedrich-Ludwig-Jahn-Sportpark, Wolliner Straße), Bus 247 (Wolliner Straße), U8 Bernauer Straße (~0.5 km), U2 Eberswalder Straße nearby.
- History: former Berlin Wall border/death strip, off-limits 1961–1989; park opened 9 November 1994 (five years after the Wall fell). Source: visitBerlin.
