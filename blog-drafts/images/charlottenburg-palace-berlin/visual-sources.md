## Quality Gate

Final contact sheet: `assets/contact-sheet/final-contact-sheet.jpg`.

Accepted set after visual check:

- Cover is topic-specific, instantly understandable, and stronger than the flat facade option.
- Image set covers exterior, gardens, interior, and satellite buildings.
- No protest/event-specific, misleading, low-resolution, or generic Berlin street images were used.
- No fabricated historical/sensitive imagery.

Rejected or not used:

- `02-palace-garden-wide.jpg`: visually attractive, but EXIF showed `copyright: Michael.F.H.Barth` and no source URL/license was available in this run. Rejected for rights uncertainty.
- `02-charlottenburg-palace-facade.jpg`: license/source clear, but visually dull for cover due to flat light and large empty sky. Kept downloaded as a reserve only, not used in the Wix draft.
- `04-charlottenburg-belvedere.jpg`: source/license clear, but the New Pavilion image is cleaner and more directly useful for the article structure.
- `05-palace-park.jpg`: source/license clear, but the final garden panorama and Mausoleum image made it redundant.
