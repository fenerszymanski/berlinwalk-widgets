# tiergarten-berlin — visual sources

All images are Wikimedia Commons, license-checked via the Commons API
(`imageinfo` + `extmetadata`) on 2026-07-12. Optimized to 1600px longest edge,
JPEG quality ~85, saved under `optimized/`. Reader-facing Image Credits block
included in the published body per the license-attribution requirement.

## Selected (5)

1. **01-tiergarten-berlin-loewenbruecke-cyclist-cover.jpg** (COVER)
   - Source: "Bicyclist, Großer Tiergarten, Berlin" by Ryan Snyder
   - License: CC BY 2.0
   - URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Bicyclist,_Gro%C3%9Fer_Tiergarten,_Berlin_(13543027693).jpg
   - Rationale: cyclist crossing an ornamental footbridge over a park
     waterway, spring blossoms, calm reflection — reads instantly as "a real
     park in central Berlin" at cover/card size, active/leisure use, strong
     composition. Chosen over a faded 1990s-era aerial scan (rejected, washed
     out/dated) and two December path/lake shots (rejected, bare branches,
     visually dull per the editorial quality gate).

2. **02-tiergarten-berlin-soviet-war-memorial.jpg**
   - Source: "Sowjetisches Ehrenmal, Tiergarten, Berlin, 170514, ako-2" by Ansgar Koreng
   - License: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Sowjetisches_Ehrenmal,_Tiergarten,_Berlin,_170514,_ako-2.jpg
   - Rationale: crisp, symmetric, blue-sky shot of the Soviet War Memorial
     colonnade and T-34 tanks — the clearest single monument photo for the
     history section.

3. **03-tiergarten-berlin-bellevue-palace.jpg**
   - Source: "Bellevue Palace Berlin 02-14" by A.Savin
   - License: CC BY-SA 3.0
   - URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Bellevue_Palace_Berlin_02-14.jpg
   - Rationale: clean frontal shot of the German President's official
     residence, instantly identifiable, good midday light.

4. **04-tiergarten-berlin-englischer-garten.jpg**
   - Source: "Berlin Tiergarten vista - IMG 8400" by Daderot
   - License: Public domain (Daderot releases his own work into the public
     domain; credited anyway per this project's usual Image Credits style)
   - URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin_Tiergarten_vista_-_IMG_8400.JPG
   - Rationale: lush late-spring English Garden planting, strong greenery,
     shows the park's gardened (not just wooded) character.

5. **05-tiergarten-berlin-loewenbruecke-lions.jpg**
   - Source: "Löwenbrücke 1 Großer Tiergarten Berlin" by Lienhard Schulz
   - License: CC BY-SA 3.0
   - URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:L%C3%B6wenbr%C3%BCcke_1_Gro%C3%9Fer_Tiergarten_Berlin.JPG
   - Rationale: the lion-guarded footbridge is a genuine "most visitors miss
     this" detail — good for the practical/insider section, autumn color adds
     visual variety against the other four images.

## Rejected

- Aerial slide-scan of Tiergarten/Soviet memorial (GeorgeLouis, CC BY-SA 3.0):
  washed-out film-scan colors, dated look, small in-frame plane-window
  shadow — fails the cover editorial bar.
- "Berlin Tiergarten Siegessäule Luftansicht.jpg": only 800x600, too low-res
  for a modern cover.
- Two December 2025 Englischer Garten / Neuer See shots (GPSLeo, Reinhold
  Möller): bare branches, muted brown palette, visually dull/uninviting.
- Two June-2015 "Gr-Tiergarten" shelter photos: shelter walls covered in
  graffiti, not representative of the park's appeal.

No AI-generated visuals were used in this package.
