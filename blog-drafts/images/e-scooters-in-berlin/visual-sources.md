# E-Scooters in Berlin — Visual Sources

All images sourced from Wikimedia Commons. Every image used carries a CC BY or
CC BY-SA licence, so a reader-facing **Image Credits** block is required in the
post body. Optimized to max 1500px, sRGB JPEG, EXIF orientation baked in.

## Used in post

1. **02-shared-scooters-lined-up-berlin.jpg** (COVER + first inline)
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Tier_scooters_in_Berlin.jpg
   - Author: Geoprofi Lars — Licence: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Why: A long row of shared kick e-scooters (TIER, now Dott + Lime) parked in
     the historic centre. Sunny, clear subject, instantly reads as the article
     topic. Chosen as cover.

2. **03-riders-and-bikes-berlin.jpg** (where you may ride section)
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Micromobility_users_in_Berlin.jpg
   - Author: Sonia Medina — Licence: CC BY 3.0
   - Why: A real rider on a stand-up e-scooter sharing road space with cyclists;
     supports the bike-lane / road vs sidewalk point.

3. **04-e-scooter-verboten-sign-park.jpg** (where you may NOT ride section)
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin_Park_am_Gleisdreieck_lub_2023-03-01_E-Scooter-Verbotsschild_img03.jpg
   - Author: Lukas Beck — Licence: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Why: A real "E-Scooter verboten!" sign in a Berlin park (Gleisdreieck).
     Concrete proof that scooter bans exist and are posted, not theoretical.

4. **05-parked-scooters-karl-liebknecht.jpg** (parking section — done right)
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:E-Scooter_in_Berlin-Mitte.jpg
   - Author: Jörg Fuhrmann (Intermerker) — Licence: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Why: TIER + Lime scooters lined up neatly at the roadside edge in the central
     boulevard core — what "parked correctly" looks like.

5. **06-toppled-scooters-berlin.jpg** (parking section — done wrong)
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:E-Scooter_nach_der_Nutzung_1.jpg
   - Author: Jörg Fuhrmann (Intermerker) — Licence: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Why: A toppled pile of TIER/Bird scooters half-blocking the pavement — the
     exact behaviour that pushed Berlin to designated parking zones and fines.
