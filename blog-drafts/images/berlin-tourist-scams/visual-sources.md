## AI Cover Prompt

Create one high-quality 16:9 editorial blog cover image for BerlinWalk, for an article titled "Berlin Tourist Scams: What to Ignore, What to Check and How to Stay Calm".

Visual direction: a calm, modern Berlin travel-safety scene, not scary and not documentary evidence. Show a recognizably Berlin transit/tourist setting inspired by Alexanderplatz or a U-Bahn/S-Bahn station: yellow train or platform cues, a visitor holding a phone/map close to the body, a zipped small bag, and a subtle background hint of a clipboard/petition approach or tourist-photo pressure, but keep it abstract and non-accusatory. The mood should say "street-smart, calm, prepared".

Style: polished editorial illustration with realistic city texture, BerlinWalk palette accents (#1B5E20 dark green, #FFE600 yellow, #7CB342 lime, #FAFAF5 cream), clean composition, strong card-crop readability, no text, no logos, no watermarks, no readable signs, no fake police insignia, no real recognizable faces, no violence, no protest/event scene, no fearmongering.
