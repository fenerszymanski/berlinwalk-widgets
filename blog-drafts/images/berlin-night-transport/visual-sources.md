# Berlin Night Transport Visual Sources

Run date: 2026-06-19 Europe/Berlin.

No paid image generation APIs or CLI image generation were used for article visuals. Final set uses four Wikimedia Commons photographs with strong topic fit. Contact sheet reviewed locally at `contact-sheet.jpg`.

## Final Images

1. `01-berlin-night-transport-alexanderplatz-tram.jpg`
   - Role: featured/cover and first body image.
   - Source: Wikimedia Commons, `File:Alexanderplatz at night with blurred moving tram.jpg`
   - URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Alexanderplatz_at_night_with_blurred_moving_tram.jpg
   - Author: domdomegg
   - License: CC BY 4.0
   - Rationale: strongest cover signal. It shows Alexanderplatz, U-Bahn signage, TV Tower context and a moving tram at night in one clean frame.
   - Quality gate: accepted. Clear subject, Berlin-specific, night-transport specific, strong card crop, not protest/event-specific.

2. `02-berlin-zoo-station-night.jpg`
   - Role: weekday/weekend night station context.
   - Source: Wikimedia Commons, `File:Berlin Zoo Station at night 2.JPG`
   - URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin_Zoo_Station_at_night_2.JPG
   - Author: Arne Huckelheim
   - License: CC BY-SA 3.0
   - Rationale: recognizable late-evening station scene, useful for "wait at main stops" and late return guidance.
   - Quality gate: accepted. Slight older-camera look but strong night station context and readable at blog width.

3. `03-sbahn-hauptbahnhof-berlin.jpg`
   - Role: S-Bahn/Hauptbahnhof arrival and main-line fallback context.
   - Source: Wikimedia Commons, `File:S-Bahn at Hauptbahnhof Berlin.JPG`
   - URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:S-Bahn_at_Hauptbahnhof_Berlin.JPG

   - Quality gate: accepted. Excellent composition, high quality, topic-specific, strong crop.

4. `04-night-bus-stop-berlin-karow.jpg`
   - Role: night bus/last-mile context.
   - Source: Wikimedia Commons, `File:Berlin Bahnhof Karow lub 2023-10-09 Bushaltestelle.jpg`
   - URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin_Bahnhof_Karow_lub_2023-10-09_Bushaltestelle.jpg

   - Quality gate: accepted. Clean, literal, low clutter, strong night bus signal.

## Rejections / Notes

- Generic daytime BVG bus photos were rejected because they did not support the after-midnight angle.
- Protest/night-train demonstration images were rejected as event-specific and misleading for a tourist transport guide.
- SVG route maps were not used as article visuals because they are informative but weak as editorial blog images.
