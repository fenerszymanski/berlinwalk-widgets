# Gendarmenmarkt — Visual Sources

Post: `gendarmenmarkt-berlin`
Sourced 2026-07-12. All images are freely licensed via Wikimedia Commons. No
paid API or AI image generation used. Germany has Freedom of Panorama, so photos
of these public buildings and the public square are fine to use. All five images
require attribution, so the post carries a reader-facing Image credits block.

## Final set (optimized/)

1. **01-...-square-konzerthaus-french-cathedral-cover.jpg** — FEATURED / cover / OG
   - Subject: Wide view across Gendarmenmarkt with the Konzerthaus on the left,
     the Schiller monument in the middle distance, and the French Cathedral
     (Französischer Dom) with its full domed tower on the right, people on the
     square, blue sky. Shows two of the three landmarks plus the plaza and human
     scale in one frame, so it reads instantly as "the square". Landscape.
   - Source: File:Berlin - Konzerthaus Berlin & Französischer Dom.jpg
   - URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin_-_Konzerthaus_Berlin_%26_Fran%C3%B6sischer_Dom.jpg
   - Author: Fred Romero · License: CC BY 2.0 (attribution required)

2. **02-...-franzosischer-dom-french-cathedral-tower.jpg** — French Cathedral section
   - Subject: The French Cathedral (Französischer Dom) domed tower in warm low
     sun, golden figure on top, colonnade. Clean, atmospheric, shows exactly
     what the paid climb sits on top of. Portrait.
   - Source: File:Französischer Dom Berlin (18838).jpg
   - URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Franz%C3%B6sischer_Dom_Berlin_(18838).jpg
   - Author: Jochen Sievert · License: CC BY-SA 4.0 (attribution required)

3. **03-...-konzerthaus-schiller-statue.jpg** — Konzerthaus / Schiller section

   - Source: File:150418 Konzerthaus Berlin Gendarmenmarkt.jpg

## Considered and rejected

- **File:Französischer Dom 9083.jpg** (Dosseman, CC BY-SA 4.0) — high resolution
  but it is a photo of an information plaque, not the building. Rejected as a
  hero image. (It does independently confirm the myth-buster: the plaque text
  states the French "Dom" is "not itself a church" and that "dôme" means cupola.)
- **File:Deutscher Dom afternoon light.jpg** (Leonhard Lenz, CC BY-SA 4.0) —
  beautiful golden-hour crop, but only the dome top, less useful than the full
  building for a "how to visit" section. Held in reserve.
- **File:Berlin - Gendarmenmarkt (tone-mapping).jpg** (Guido Radig, CC BY 3.0) —
  good composition but heavy HDR tone-mapping gives the paving an artificial
  purple cast. Rejected in favour of the cleaner Fred Romero cover.
