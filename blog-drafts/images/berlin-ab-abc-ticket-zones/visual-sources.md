## Rejected Or Demoted

- Generic train-platform photos without a recognizable Berlin signal were rejected.
- Route-map screenshots were rejected as article visuals because they are informative but weak editorial assets and can become stale.
- Protest, crowd-disruption, strike, or event-specific transport photos were rejected as misleading for a normal tourist ticket guide.
- Ticket-machine closeups with unreadable screens were rejected except the selected machine pair, which is clear enough as support.
