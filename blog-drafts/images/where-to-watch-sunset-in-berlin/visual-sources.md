strength (strong light, clear subject, instantly readable at card size) and
verified licensing. Raw files in `raw/`, optimized (long edge 1600px, JPEG q80)
in `optimized/`. No AI-generated or code-drawn visuals used.

## Cover choice
01 (Oberbaumbrücke) is the featured/cover image: dramatic golden sky, the
sun starring through the arch, the iconic double-decker bridge and a yellow
U-Bahn — instantly "Berlin" and instantly "sunset" at listing-card size.

## Notes / honesty
- 04 (Viktoriapark) is a daytime view; used inline to show the elevated vantage
  point, captioned as the view, not as a sunset.
- 05 (Drachenberg) is soft twilight ("Morgendämmerung" original); captioned
  honestly as the wide western panorama and its colour, not claimed as sunset.
- All five require attribution → reader-facing Image Credits block at the
  foot of the post.

## Rejected
- Various generic "Berlin skyline" / non-Berlin sunset files from Commons search
  (Bodensee, Cape Soya, etc.) — off-topic.
- Second Oberbaumbrücke close-ups — avoided to prevent repetition with the cover;
  chose the wider Molecule Man/Spree composition (03) instead.
