## Quality gate notes
- Cover (Cosmonaut) chosen over an East Side Gallery hero on purpose: the post's
  angle is "there is far more than the Wall," so the hero is a real
  neighbourhood mural, not the Wall itself. Clean, iconic, landscape, blue sky.
- East Side Gallery "Kiss" kept as an inline (it is the single most famous
  piece, and gets its own dedicated guide linked from the post).
- Rejected: original Haus Schwarzenberg close-up (`...02.jpg`) — authentic but a
  cluttered paste-up collage with a small political sticker; swapped for the
  atmospheric confirmed-location Rosenthaler 39 wall (Babewyn) instead.
- Rejected: marsupium screaming-face stencil — striking but location
  unconfirmed as Haus Schwarzenberg, and too dark/intense for the slot.
- Urban Nation facade shot is a touch grey (winter) but it is the clearest,
  correctly-located view of the free museum, so it earns the slot on information
  value.
