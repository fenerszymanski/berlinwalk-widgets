## Source notes for factual claims

- Berlin.de City Tax overview: https://www.berlin.de/tourismus/infos/3298255-1721039-city-tax-fuer-berlintouristen-tritt-in-k.html
  - Used for: private overnight stays since 2014; business stays included from 1 April 2024; 7.5% of net overnight price excluding extras such as breakfast; hotels collect it on the invoice.
- Berlin Senate Finance City Tax downloads: https://www.berlin.de/sen/finanzen/steuern/downloads/artikel.57924.php
  - Used for: current official City Tax law/download area and 2026 procedural context.
- visitBerlin Convention Office City Tax page: https://convention.visitberlin.de/en/city-tax-berlin
  - Used for: English-language explanation that the tax applies to paid overnight stays, private and business stays, hotels/guesthouses/similar accommodation; 2026 procedural changes; rate remains 7.5%.
