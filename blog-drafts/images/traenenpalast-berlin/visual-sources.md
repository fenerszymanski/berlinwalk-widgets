- `modern-exterior-msu1231` and `modern-exterior-msu1253`: strong night exteriors, but too similar to the selected cover for a five-image article.
- `spree-context-2024`: good location context, but the subject is less prominent and weaker at article-card crop.
- `museum-booth`: mislabeled in the quick sheet and visually weaker than the selected entrance/sign images.
- Older 2004/2008 exterior files: lower resolution or pre-renovation/event-hall context, so they risk confusing a practical visitor guide.
