Selected visual set: 5 Wikimedia Commons images, no paid image/video API use, no AI article visuals. Contact sheet checked at `assets/contact-sheet/bebelplatz-berlin-contact-sheet.jpg`.

## Rejection Notes

- Low-resolution Bundesarchiv/old opera images were rejected for article visuals because they are too small or archival for the tourist-intent guide.
- Night and snow images were available, including CC0 files, but rejected as cover candidates because they were too dark or seasonal.
- Temporary-artwork images were rejected because they could mislead readers about what is normally on Bebelplatz.
