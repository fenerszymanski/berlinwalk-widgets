## Rejection notes

- Rejected protest/event-specific Brandenburg Gate images because they would mislead the card context.
- Rejected cluttered or low-resolution memorial snapshots where the stelae were hard to read at listing-card size.
- Rejected Information Centre search results without clear reusable image metadata.
- No generated, staged or reconstructed Holocaust/history scenes were used.
