# Visual sources — Berlin Train Announcements

All images from Wikimedia Commons, downloaded 2026-07-14 at 1600px, re-encoded (sips q70).
Every image is a real Berlin U-Bahn/S-Bahn train or platform (topic-accurate). No AI visuals used.
Licenses require reader-facing credit → Image Credits block in the post body.

## Used in post (5)

1. `01-sbahn-alexanderplatz-cover.jpg` — COVER + top-of-body
   - Source: File:Berlin S-Bahn Bhf Alexanderplatz (S07 1302).jpg
   - URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin_S-Bahn_Bhf_Alexanderplatz_(S07_1302).jpg
   - Author: Michael.F.H.Barth — License: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Why: red/yellow S-Bahn arriving under the Alexanderplatz glass roof; the exact hub where
     visitors ride in for the tour and hear their first announcements. Strong, clean, topic-perfect.

2. `02-sbahn-s7-destination.jpg` — "which train / Zug nach" section
   - Source: File:Berlin- Bahnhof Friedrichstraße- auf Bahnsteig zu Gleis 5- DBAG-Baureihe 481 7.8.2009.JPG
   - URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin-_Bahnhof_Friedrichstra%C3%9Fe-_auf_Bahnsteig_zu_Gleis_5-_DBAG-Baureihe_481_7.8.2009.JPG
   - Author: Jivee Blau — License: CC BY-SA 3.0
   - Why: classic S-Bahn with the front reading "S7 Ahrensfelde" (line + destination). Shows exactly
     what "Zug nach [destination]" means; doors open, real passengers, Friedrichstraße.

3. `03-ubahn-departure-board.jpg` — "two voices / the display" section
   - Source: File:Berliner U-Bahnhof Innsbrucker Platz (Zug Richtung Nollendorfplatz).jpg
   - URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berliner_U-Bahnhof_Innsbrucker_Platz_(Zug_Richtung_Nollendorfplatz).jpg
   - Author: Standardizer — License: CC BY-SA 3.0
   - Why: iconic yellow BVG U-Bahn AND an overhead board reading "Linie / Ziel / Abfahrt — U4
     Nollendorfplatz in 2 min — Gleis 2". Teaches the display vocabulary in one frame.

4. `04-new-sbahn-interior-display.jpg` — "Nächster Halt / new trains / jingle" section
   - Source: File:Berlin S-Bahn 483-484 Innenraum.jpg
   - URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Berlin_S-Bahn_483-484_Innenraum.jpg
   - Author: Geoprofi Lars — License: CC BY-SA 4.0
   - Why: interior of the new 483/484 S-Bahn with the onboard strip reading "S47 Hermannstraße" —
     the same line + destination the recorded voice reads out. Also the new train that beeps
     instead of the old three-note jingle.

5. `05-alexanderplatz-platform.jpg` — closing / tour tie-in
   - Source: File:S-Bahnsteig Bahnhof Berlin-Alexanderplatz 2023.jpg

## Considered and rejected
- File:DB-Baureihe 483 - 484 offene Tür.jpg (CC BY-SA 4.0, Geoprofi Lars): great open-door shot of
  the new train, but a prominent red "Einstieg nur mit Maske / Wear a face covering" sticker dates it
  to 2020-2021 and could mislead 2026 readers about mask rules. Rejected as misleading.
- Numerous other Michael.F.H.Barth Ringbahn/S-Bahn station shots: fine but redundant with the 5 above.
