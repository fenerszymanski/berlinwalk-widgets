## Quality Gate

Final contact sheet: `assets/contact-sheet/final-contact-sheet.jpg`.

Accepted set after visual check:

- Cover is topic-specific, instantly understandable, and readable at card crop.
- Set covers the booking decisions in the article: Reichstag, TV Tower, museums, Berlin palace visit and Potsdam/Sanssouci.
- No weak, dull, cluttered, protest/event-specific, misleading or generic street-scene image was used.
- No fabricated historical or sensitive imagery.

Rejected or not used:

- Commons search results for generic restaurant/reservation queries returned irrelevant PDFs and weak generic material; rejected for poor relevance.
- Boat-tour image candidate was usable but less central to the article's decision hierarchy than the selected palace/Sanssouci images.
- Additional Reichstag interior images were not used because the exterior dome image already carried the strongest booking signal.
