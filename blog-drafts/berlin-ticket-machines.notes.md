## Fares verified 2026 (VBB / berlin.de / bvg.de, checked 2026-07-14)
- Single AB 4.00 (red. 2.50), valid 120 min, one direction, changes allowed
- Single ABC 5.00 (red. 3.50) — needed for BER airport (Zone C)
- Kurzstrecke 2.80 (red. 2.10) — 3 U/S-Bahn stops (1 change) or 6 bus/tram stops
- Day ticket AB 11.20 (red. 7.40), until 3:00 next morning
- Day ticket ABC 12.90 (red. 8.00)
- 7-day travelcard discontinued 1 Jan 2026
- Machines: ~500 on S-Bahn platforms, touchscreen DE/EN/FR/TR/ES; coins 5c-2€,
  notes 5/10/20€, girocard/Visa/Mastercard/Maestro/V-Pay, usually Apple/Google Pay;
  limited change. Paper ticket must be stamped before boarding; fine 60€.
- Trams: onboard coin machine; buses: driver.
