## Official Research Sources

- BVG ticket control: https://www.bvg.de/en/subscriptions-and-tickets/ticket-control
  - Valid ticket required during control.
  - Standard EBE is EUR 60 without valid ticket.
  - Personalized BVG subscription forgotten: proof within 7 days can reduce to EUR 7 processing.
  - EBE receipt number is important.
- BVG tourist ticket guidance: https://www.bvg.de/en/tourists/tickets
  - Paper tickets must be validated before the journey.
  - Digital tickets must be active before travel.
  - Single ticket is valid for 120 minutes after validation in one direction.
- BVG EBE portal: https://bvg-ebe.de/en-en
  - Pay/check EBE with the EBE number.
  - Access generally available from noon on the next working day.
  - Missing the deadline adds costs.
- S-Bahn Berlin ticket control: https://sbahn.berlin/en/tickets/the-vbb-fare-explained/ticket-control/
  - EUR 60 EBE, usually payable within 14 days if not paid on site.
  - Confirmation receipt and transaction number matter.
  - Forgotten personal ticket proof can be submitted within seven days.
  - Warning about fake inspectors and cash demands.
  - EBE is a civil claim, not automatically a criminal fine.
- Berlin.de tickets and route maps: https://www.berlin.de/en/public-transportation/1772016-2913840-tickets-fares-and-route-maps.en.html
  - Tickets must be validated before journey.
  - Unstamped tickets are invalid.
  - Fare evasion EBE is EUR 60 and tourists are not exempt.
