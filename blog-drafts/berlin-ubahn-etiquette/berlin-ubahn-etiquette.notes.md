## Verified facts (sources checked 2026-07-19)
- Escalator: stand right, walk left ("rechts stehen, links gehen"). germanculture / berlintourism.
- Let people off before boarding ("Einsteigen lassen").
- Doors: many U-Bahn/S-Bahn doors open only when you press the illuminated button / lift handle. (bvg door-button behaviour; allaboutberlin, medium idiot's guide.)
- Priority seats reserved for elderly/disabled/pregnant/with children (icon-marked).
- Eating: no formal BVG food ban, but hot/greasy/smelly food discouraged; alcohol technically allowed on U/S-Bahn but frowned on. Source: germanculture, bvg usage-policy.
- Bikes: need own bike ticket; not allowed weekday rush hours ~6–9am & 2–5pm; board at bike-symbol door, ~2 bikes/area; folded bike free. Source: bvg.de/en/bicycle.
- Tickets: open platforms, no barriers, plain-clothes inspectors, €60 fine. (Existing post: /post/berlin-u-bahn-fine.)
