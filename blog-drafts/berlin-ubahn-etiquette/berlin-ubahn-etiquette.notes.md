## Verified facts (primary sources checked 2026-07-20)
- Escalator: stand right and leave the left clear ("rechts stehen, links gehen") is a strong local convention.
- Let people off before boarding: "Erst aussteigen lassen, dann einsteigen."
- Doors: many U-Bahn/S-Bahn doors open only when the passenger uses the illuminated button or older handle.
- Priority seats reserved for elderly/disabled/pregnant/with children (icon-marked).
- Food and drink: VBB conditions §4(2)11 prohibit entering vehicles with open food/open drinks and consuming them during the journey.
- Alcohol: the same open-drink rule applies on board. A DB pilot from 1 May 2026 additionally prohibits alcohol consumption/open containers at Zoologischer Garten and Ostbahnhof, including forecourts, outside licensed venues.
- Bikes: normally require a bike ticket; use marked areas; wheelchair users and prams have priority; there is no guaranteed carriage when full. Current BVG/VBB guidance has no blanket weekday rush-hour ban. BVG buses do not carry bikes.
- Tickets: open platforms have no barriers, but inspectors check. Travelling without a valid ticket can lead to a €60 EBE receipt/notice; payment is handled using its number rather than necessarily on the spot.
