Where to Watch the World Cup in Berlin

The 2026 World Cup is played in North America, but Berlin is full of places to watch. Free public screens, beer gardens and bars — filter by what you're after.

No Brandenburg Gate fan mile this year. For the first time since 2006, Berlin skipped its famous Fanmeile. Most kick-offs are evening or overnight Berlin time; the city allows outdoor screening for matches that start by 10 pm, so very late games move indoors.
