How are you getting there?

BER is in fare zone C. From central Berlin, most visitors need an ABC ticket unless their pass already covers zone C.

Comfortable plan

Reach BER about 2 hours before departure.

This is enough for a normal Schengen flight with hand baggage when you have a clean route and your airline does not require extra desk time.

3h 00m Before flight: leave central Berlin
2h 00m Before flight: be at the terminal
ASAP After bag drop: go to security

Train plan

Check FEX, regional trains and S-Bahn live in BVG, VBB or DB Navigator. Do not trust yesterday's screenshot during construction work.

Security habit

Pack liquids as 100 ml containers in a 1-liter bag. If you get a newer CT lane, it only makes the process easier.

Terminal 2

Terminal 2 is walkable from Terminal 1, but add a small buffer if you have luggage, children or a tight boarding time.

After BerlinWalk
