Which Berlin open-air cinema is right for tonight?

Berlin has more than a dozen open-air screens in summer. Pick what matters to you and this shows the ones that fit, with the nearest station and how to check that night's language.

Reading the listings: OV is the original language, so English for most big films. OmU is the original language with German subtitles (still English audio). OmeU adds English subtitles. Avoid a plain German dub, usually shown with no code.

What matters to you tonight?

No open-air cinema matches every filter at once. Try removing one, for example keep Films in English but drop Skyline view.

Season runs roughly early May to mid-September, with films starting after dark (often not before 21:30 in high summer). Tickets are usually about 9 to 12 euros. Always confirm the date's language code and any weather cancellation on the cinema's own page before you travel.
