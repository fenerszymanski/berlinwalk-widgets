Olympiastadion: is it even open, and how should you see it?

The catch nobody warns you about: on Hertha home games and big events the visitor centre shuts and you cannot just walk in. Set the day, then pick how you want to visit.

Green areas are what you reach on your chosen visit. Faded areas stay off-limits.

Prices and hours are 2026 guidance from the official visitor centre and can change at short notice. Always check olympiastadion.berlin for your date before travelling out.
