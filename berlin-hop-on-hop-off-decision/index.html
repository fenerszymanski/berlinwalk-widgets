Hop-on hop-off is shown as a mid-range price across Berlin's main operators (roughly €30–€45 per adult for 24 hours); exact fares, child prices and multi-day passes vary by operator. Bus 100/200 is a normal BVG bus covered by any valid ticket, so its cost is just the €11.20 AB day ticket. The walking-tour line is tip-based, so what you pay is up to you.
