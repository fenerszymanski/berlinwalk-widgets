## Visual Direction

- Style: cinematic photorealistic archive-thriller imagery, premium but readable.
- Core motif: an evidence desk in Berlin, with paper files, map fragments, old photo prints, subtle East/West boundary cues, pins, stamps, and BerlinWalk green/yellow accents.
- Palette: deep BerlinWalk green `#1B5E20`, yellow `#FFE600`, lime `#7CB342`, cream `#FAFAF5`, text black `#212121`, tiny Berlin red `#E63946` only for warning pins/stamps.
