Easy family plan

Museum, playground, short walk

A balanced first Berlin day for school-age kids: one big indoor win, one movement break, then a short historic walk if energy stays good.

Ticket note

Children under 6 do not need a Berlin public transport ticket. Ages 6 to 14 use reduced fare.

Break rule

Plan one snack or playground pause every 90 minutes. Berlin days fail when adults try to stack too many museums.

Backup

If energy drops, stop after the first two blocks and save the landmark walk for tomorrow morning.
