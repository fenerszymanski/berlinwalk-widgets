The Perfect Last Day in Berlin

Your final hours have one job: leave Berlin without rushing. Store the bags, choose one central stop, and start the airport or station move earlier than feels necessary.

The only order that works

Do the day in this order: luggage, one close final stop, direct transit, check-in or platform, then snacks. Reverse it and the last hour gets stressful.

4Lock the bufferEnter BER or reach the platform before any extra plan.
