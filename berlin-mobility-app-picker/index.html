Berlin Mobility Map Logic

Use the zone, then the vehicle

Central core

Walk, U-Bahn, S-Bahn, tram and bus are usually the cleanest answer around Alexanderplatz, Museum Island, Hackescher Markt and Brandenburg Gate.

Station edge

Jelbi hubs and nearby shared vehicles are useful for the last kilometre after the main train leg.

Outer trips

Check public transport first. Car sharing helps only when driving and ending the rental are clearly easy.

No-go shortcut

No e-scooters on sidewalks or pedestrian zones. If that is the only route that feels safe, choose another mode.
