Quick Berlin note before you arrive on `${arrivalDate}`.

Two things catch first-time visitors all the time:

**1. Sunday and public-holiday logistics**  
Most normal supermarkets close. Train-station shops, Spatis, restaurants, transport, parks, and outdoor sights still work, but errands become harder.

**2. Monday museum logic**  
Many museums close on Mondays. If your plan depends on one museum, check it before you build the whole day around it.
