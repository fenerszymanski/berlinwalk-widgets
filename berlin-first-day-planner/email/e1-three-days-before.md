If you are arriving at BER Airport, remember that the airport is in Zone C, so you normally need an ABC ticket before boarding. If you are starting from Hauptbahnhof, Alexanderplatz, or a hotel inside the city, AB is usually enough.

For luggage, keep the first day simple. If check-in is not ready, drop bags before sightseeing. Berlin is much easier when your hands are free and you are not dragging a suitcase across crossings, cobbles, or station stairs.

Your first target is not "all of Berlin." It is one clean central setup:
