    Berlin Wall Memorial

    Best for understanding the Wall as a physical border system with death strip, documentation and outdoor remains.

    Checkpoint Charlie

    Best when you need the famous photo stop, but it works better with context from a quieter site.

    East Side Gallery

    Best for murals and open-air Wall photos, not for the clearest explanation of how the border worked.
