Wannsee Shore Planner

Wannsee is one name for four different days: a sand beach, an island of peacocks, a villa shore with heavy history, and a ferry across the Havel. They sit on different corners of the lake, and two of them do not even start at Wannsee station. Pick what you want, and I will put it in an order that works on the ground.
