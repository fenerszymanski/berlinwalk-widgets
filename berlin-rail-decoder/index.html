Berlin Rail Decoder

A U, an S, an M and a three-digit number all sit on the same map. Type any line label and I will tell you exactly what it is, whether your ticket covers it, and when it stops running.

Tip: the letter in front of the number is the whole secret. U and S are trains, M is usually a tram, and a bare three-digit number is a bus.
