Use the ATM, but read the fee first.

A bank-branded ATM with a clear euro withdrawal screen is usually fine. Your own bank may still charge a foreign withdrawal fee, so avoid making tiny repeat withdrawals.

    Confirm in this order

    1. Place Prefer a bank ATM when you are calm, not a random late-night machine.
    2. Fee Check the operator fee before you continue.
    3. Currency For non-euro cards, reject home-currency conversion and continue in euros.
    4. Amount Carry a modest cash buffer, not your whole trip budget.
