Berlin's real beer answer is a garden, not a tent

Prater Garten

Kastanienallee 7-9 · U2 Eberswalder Straße

Berlin's oldest beer garden, in the same courtyard since 1837. Chestnut trees, long benches, grilled chicken, no dirndl.

Café am Neuen See

Tiergarten · S/U Zoologischer Garten

Beer garden on the lake inside Tiergarten, running since 1896. Rowing boats in season, Bavarian food if you want it.

Zollpackhof

Elisabeth-Abegg-Straße 1 · S/U Hauptbahnhof

Augustiner from wooden barrels with the Chancellery across the Spree. The easiest one to reach from the main station.

A Berliner Weisse anywhere

Order it "mit Schuss"

Sour wheat beer with raspberry or woodruff syrup. Bright red or alarming green, low alcohol, very much a Berlin thing.
