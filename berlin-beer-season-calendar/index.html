    Outside event dates

    Use a beer garden when the weather and opening hours allow

    Prater Garten

    Kastanienallee 7-9 · U2 Eberswalder Straße

    Long benches under chestnut trees. Check the same-day opening notice because the garden depends on the weather.

    Café am Neuen See

    Tiergarten · S/U Zoologischer Garten

    A lake-side garden inside Tiergarten, with rowing boats in season and Bavarian food on the menu.

    Zollpackhof

    Elisabeth-Abegg-Straße 1 · S/U Hauptbahnhof

    Augustiner beer with the Chancellery across the Spree, close to the main station.

    Berliner Weisse mit Schuss

    Raspberry red · woodruff green

    A tart, usually low-alcohol Berlin wheat beer. Order the syrup by colour if the German name is difficult.

    Dates checked 20 July 2026 against the Berlin events calendar and the named organizers. Programmes, tickets and opening hours can change, so confirm the exact event before travelling.
