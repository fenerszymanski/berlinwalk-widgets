Can I smoke here? Check the Berlin rule before you light up

Berlin lets you smoke in some places many visitors do not expect, while transport and cannabis rules stay strict. Pick what you are using and where you are for practical, current guidance.

1

What are you using?

ℹ️ Possession and legal supply are different. Adults 18 and over may carry up to 25 g in public, but Germany has no general retail cannabis shops. Cultivation associations require at least six months of German residence and a minimum three-month membership, so short-term visitors cannot use that route. Street sales remain illegal.
