Can I smoke here? Check the Berlin rule before you light up

Berlin lets you smoke in some places most cities do not, and fines you in a few that feel harmless. Pick what you are using, then pick where you are, and get the exact rule, the likely fine, and one practical move.

1

What are you using?

ℹ️ Possession is legal, buying is not for visitors. Adults 18 and over may carry up to 25 g in public, but there are no cannabis shops in Germany. The only legal supply is non-profit cannabis clubs that require German residence, so as a tourist you cannot legally buy it. Street dealers stay illegal.
