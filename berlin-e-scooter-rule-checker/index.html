The 5 rules that actually matter

  • One rider per scooter. No passengers, ever.
  • No pavements, no pedestrian zones. Use the bike lane, or the road if there's none.
  • Max 20 km/h, and you must be at least 14 to ride (apps usually want 18+ to rent).
  • Park at the kerb edge or in a marked bay. Never block the pavement.
  • Alcohol counts as drink-driving. It's a motor vehicle in the eyes of the law.
