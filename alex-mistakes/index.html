⚠️ What Tourists Do vs. What You Should Do
❌ What tourists do
Stand under the TV Tower and shoot straight up
✅ What you should do
Walk to Liebknecht Bridge for the best photo — TV Tower + Dom + Humboldt Forum in one frame
❌ What tourists do
Eat at the chain restaurants on the square
✅ What you should do
Walk 5 min to Hackescher Markt or Rosenthaler Platz for real food
❌ What tourists do
Think the square is ugly and move on
✅ What you should do
Learn why the GDR designed it this way — it's one of Berlin's best Cold War stories
❌ What tourists do
Use Alexanderplatz only as a transit stop
✅ What you should do
Spend 30 minutes exploring — this is where Berlin began 800 years ago
❌ What tourists do
Skip the World Clock without understanding it
✅ What you should do
Read the Cold War propaganda story behind it — more interesting than the TV Tower
