🏆 Best Month to Visit Berlin — Quick Answer
For most visitors, May, June, and September offer the best combination of warm weather (19–23°C), long daylight hours, and manageable crowds. If you only have one choice: pick June — 17 hours of daylight, 23°C average, and Berlin at its most alive.
