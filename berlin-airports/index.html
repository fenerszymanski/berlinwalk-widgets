Rail leaves from Flughafen BER – Terminal 1-2, the station directly under both terminals. BER is in fare zone C, so you need a Berlin ABC ticket (€5.00 single in 2026). Buy it from a BVG/VBB machine or app before boarding, and stamp a paper ticket on the platform.
