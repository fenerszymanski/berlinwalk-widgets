Germany's official numbers are 112 and 110, so do not rely on 911. Start with 112 for medical or fire emergencies and 110 for police danger; the services coordinate when needed.

Not sure? Pick what is happening

For fire, serious injury or a life-threatening medical emergency, call 112. For immediate danger or a crime requiring police, call 110.
