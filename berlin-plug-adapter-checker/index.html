Start with your plug and device.

The safest Berlin charging plan is usually a Type F travel adapter plus a quick voltage-label check on anything that heats, spins or has medical importance.

    Berlin socket Germany uses Type F / Schuko sockets with 230V, 50Hz power.
    Adapter vs converter An adapter changes the plug shape. A converter changes voltage. They are not the same thing.
    Buy in Berlin Try airport shops, big stations, electronics stores or larger drugstores. Hotel desks sometimes lend one.
    Tip: if your charger label says "Input: 100-240V, 50/60Hz", you usually need only the right plug shape for Berlin.
