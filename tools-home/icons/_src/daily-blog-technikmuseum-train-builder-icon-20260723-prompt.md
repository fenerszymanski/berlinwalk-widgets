Same fixed 3-layer BerlinTools family as the other `daily-blog-*` icons:

2. solid deep green #1B5E20 rounded-square glossy 3D app tile filling the frame
3. one large centered bright yellow #FFE600 glossy circular medallion disc
4. ONE clear glossy soft-3D symbolic object resting on the yellow disc

Approved non-paid route: Yusuf's ChatGPT account through the connected Chrome
browser (share-link capture). No paid image API. Keep this production
provenance internal and out of public copy.

Subject: the tool plans a Deutsches Technikmuseum visit, and the single
unmistakable object is the museum's own landmark: the silver C-47 plane
perched on top of the glass building. No other BerlinTools icon owns a
plane-on-a-building combo; a lone plane was rejected (would collide with any
airport tool) and a lone locomotive was rejected (collides with train/station
icons). The plane resting on a roof reads as "that museum" instantly.
