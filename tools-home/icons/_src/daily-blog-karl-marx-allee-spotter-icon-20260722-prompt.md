## `karl-marx-allee-spotter` (Karl-Marx-Allee Facade Spotter)

```text
Create ONE square 1:1 app icon for BerlinWalk, in a strict fixed style. STRUCTURE, exactly three layers and nothing else: (1) a plain cream background; (2) a solid deep green #1B5E20 rounded-square app tile with soft glossy 3D bevel that fills the frame; (3) one large centered bright yellow #FFE600 glossy circular medallion disc on the tile. Resting on the yellow disc is ONE clear glossy soft-3D object: the twin towers of Frankfurter Tor in Berlin as a gate pair, two identical slender cream-white towers standing side by side, each with a small colonnade ring near the top and a rounded verdigris green copper dome with a thin spire. Show them as one chunky simplified sculptural pair, slightly angled toward each other like a city gate. No boulevard, no cars, no map, no scenery, no trees, no scattered elements, no second disc. Colors: green tile #1B5E20, yellow disc #FFE600, towers in cream white stone with warm highlights, domes in soft verdigris green, soft lime #7CB342 shadow under the towers. No text, no letters, no numbers, no logos. Do NOT use a white, glass, frosted, or pale tile. Chunky simple shapes, high contrast, centered, readable at 160x160, clean.
```

Motif rationale: the Frankfurter Tor twin domed towers, shown as a PAIR, so the
icon reads as a gate and does not collide with the single-tower Spandau
citadel icon (`spandau-old-town-loop`) or the GDR watchtower
(`berlin-wall-trail-sections`).
