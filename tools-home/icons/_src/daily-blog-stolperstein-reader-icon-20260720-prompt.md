## Prompt

grooves. Colors: green tile #1B5E20, yellow disc #FFE600, plaque in warm
polished brass/gold with soft cream highlights, paving hint in muted grey with
lime #7CB342 shading. No text, no letters, no numbers, no logos. Do NOT use a
white, glass, frosted, or pale tile. Chunky simple shapes, high contrast,
centered, readable at 160x160, clean.
