A full circle is about an hour, so a day ticket (Zone AB, 11.20 euros in 2026) is the honest choice for riding the whole ring. A single ticket is not valid for a round trip.

The Ring wraps around the historic core, but it does not go through it. For the old centre I walk on foot, I run a free walking tour from Alexanderplatz, about 2 hours.
