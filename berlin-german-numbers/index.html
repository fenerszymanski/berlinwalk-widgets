Prices: the comma is the cents

A German price tag uses a comma, not a dot, and the euros and cents are often said as two numbers with no "and" between them.

3,50 €
drei fünfzig
"three fifty" = 3 euros 50 cents
4,00 €
vier Euro
a single AB ticket in 2026
2,80 €
zwei achtzig
the Kurzstrecke short hop
0,25 €
fünfundzwanzig Cent
the Pfand on a can or bottle
