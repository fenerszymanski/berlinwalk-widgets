With an EU, EEA or Swiss passport, the Schengen 90/180 cap does not apply. For a short Berlin visit, bring a valid passport or national ID card. A stay longer than three months can involve residence conditions and local registration, so check the official EU residence guidance before a longer move.

How this works: the counter checks every day of your planned stay, looks back 180 days from that day, and counts unique Schengen days from the exact entry and exit periods you add. Do not enter time covered by an EU residence permit or long-stay D visa. If a short-stay visa sticker authorises fewer than 90 days, the sticker controls and this result does not apply.
