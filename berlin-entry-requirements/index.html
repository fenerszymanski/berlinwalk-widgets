With an EU, EEA or Swiss passport the 90/180 rule does not apply to you. You have freedom of movement and can stay in Germany as long as you like, so the day counter is only relevant for the other two passport groups.

How this works: the counter adds your Berlin trip length to any other recent Schengen days and compares the total to the 90-day allowance. The real 180-day window is a rolling one, so if you travel in and out of Europe a lot, treat this as a close guide and check the official source before a long stay.
